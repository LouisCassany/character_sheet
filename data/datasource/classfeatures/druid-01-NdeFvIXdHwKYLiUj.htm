You feel a kinship with flames and can use them for succor and destruction. You're trained in Acrobatics. You also gain the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.yOloZIGkulrrPYG4]{Fire Lung} druid feat. You gain the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.jks2h5pMsm8pCi8e]{Wildfire} order spell.
