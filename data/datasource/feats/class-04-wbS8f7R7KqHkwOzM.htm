Name: Sacral Lord Nom: Seigneur sacré État: libre -- Desc (en) --

By right of ritual or sheer stubbornness, you have claimed rulership of the land. Claim a defined territory, such as a small section of forest or river, or a district of a larger city. Within its borders, you can cast @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.gpzpAAAJ1Lza2JVl]{Detect Magic}, @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.izcxFQFwf3woCnFs]{Guidance}, and @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.AUctDF2fqPZN2w4W]{Sigil} as innate divine cantrips, and you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.OX4fy22hQgUHDr0q]{Make an Impression} on creatures that have the celestial, fiend, fey, monitor, or spirit traits.

Special You and your land are linked. Changes to one mirror themselves on the other-if you're depressed, gray rain and clammy mist might set in. These effects have no immediate mechanical impact, but they might give clues about problems afflicting the land.
