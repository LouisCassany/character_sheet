Name: Communal Healing Nom: Guérison collective État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You're an exceptional conduit for vitality and, as you channel it through you, you can divert some to heal yourself or another creature. When you cast the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.rfZpqmj0AIIdkVIs]{Heal} spell to heal a single creature, choose another willing living creature within range of your heal to regain Hit Points equal to the heal spell's rank.
