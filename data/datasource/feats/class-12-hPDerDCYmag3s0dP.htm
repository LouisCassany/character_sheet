Requirements You are wielding a shield.

Once you've had a moment to set your stance, you always have your shield ready without a thought. While you are in this stance, you constantly have your shield raised as if you'd used the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.xjGwis0uaC2305pm]{Raise a Shield} action.
