Name: Improved Command Corpse Nom: Contrôle amélioré des cadavres État: libre -- Desc (en) --

When you use @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.xYakFeP6olBsxpZN]{Command Undead} on a mindless undead, if the undead succeeds at its save but doesn't critically succeed, it becomes your minion for 1 round. If the undead fails its save, it becomes your minion for 1 hour. If it critically fails, it becomes your minion for 24 hours.
