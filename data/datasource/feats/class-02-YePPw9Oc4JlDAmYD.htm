You create a miasma of spores. These spores fill a @Template[burst|distance:20] within 100 feet and remain for 1 round per level you have. All creatures within the spores become @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.DmAIPqOBomZ7H95W]{Concealed}, and all creatures outside the spores become concealed to creatures within it. You can see through the spores without difficulty. You can Dismiss the spores.

Awakening The spores smell disgusting. Creatures in the area are @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.fesd1n5eVhpCSS18]{Sickened 1} for as long as they remain in the area, plus 2 rounds.

Awakening The spores are toxic. The miasma deals @Damage[(floor(@actor.level/2))[poison]] damage per 2 levels you have to creatures in the area when you Release Spores, to creatures who begin their turn in the area, and to creatures who enter the area.
