It's said that the flesh of the right side of a hyena can heal diseases, but that the flesh of the left side is deadly and poisonous. You can deal @Damage[1[slashing]] damage to yourself to feed someone blood from your right side and Administer First Aid or take @Damage[2d8[untyped]] damage to @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.TC7OcDa7JlWbqMaN]{Treat Disease} or @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.1kGNdIIhuglAjIp9]{Treat Wounds}; in either case, you don't need a healer's toolkit, and you gain a +1 item bonus to your check. Blood from your left side causes the check to critically fail automatically.
