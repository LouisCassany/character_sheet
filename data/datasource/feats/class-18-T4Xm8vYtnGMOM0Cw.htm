Name: Echoing Channel Nom: Canalisation répétée État: libre -- Desc (en) --

When you pull forth vitality or void energy, you also create a smaller pocket of that energy. If the next action you use is to cast a 2-action @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.wdA52JJnsuQWeyqz]{Harm} or @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.rfZpqmj0AIIdkVIs]{Heal} to heal or damage a single creature, choose one additional creature in range. Target that creature with a 1-action version of the same spell. This spell is the same rank as the 2-action harm or heal you cast and doesn't cost another spell slot.
