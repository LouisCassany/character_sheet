Name: Order Magic Nom: Magie des ordres État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You have delved deeper into the teaching of a new order, gaining access to a coveted order spell. Choose an order you have selected with @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.9onf3uGvZRnNLPR6]{Order Explorer}. You gain the initial order spell from that order.

Special You can take this feat multiple times. Each time you do, you must choose a different order you have selected with Order Explorer.
