While in @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.RsNvCSrCN7czHC0G]{Ricochet Stance}, you can bounce a thrown weapon off a foe to distract them. You can attempt a @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.QNAVeNKtHA0EUw4X]{Feint} against a creature within the first range increment of a thrown weapon you are wielding, rather than only creatures within your melee reach.
