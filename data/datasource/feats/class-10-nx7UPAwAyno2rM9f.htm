Name: Thaumaturge's Investiture Nom: Investiture du thaumaturge État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Magical equipment and gear are the tools of your trade, and you know you need as many as possible to help you against the supernatural. You gain the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.4vu6P3cYoOOeUbLK]{Incredible Investiture} skill feat, increasing your limit on invested items from 10 to 12. The limit increases to 14 if you have Charisma +4, 16 if you have Charisma +5, 18 if you have Charisma +6, and 20 if you have Charisma +7.
