Requirements You are wielding two melee weapons, each in a different hand.

You refine the precision of your unbelievable storm of blows.

When you perform an @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.OqHfUQQorVBkx34j]{Impossible Flurry}, your first attack with each weapon instead takes the multiple attack penalty as though you had already made one attack this turn.
