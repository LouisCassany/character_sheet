Name: Flamboyant Athlete Nom: Athlète flamboyant État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Your Panache allows you to perform incredible feats: climbing, swimming, and leaping far beyond your normal capacity. While you have panache, you gain the following benefits.

  • You gain climb and swim Speeds equal to half your land Speed.
  • The DCs of your @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.2HJ4yuEFY1Cast4h]{High Jumps} and @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.JUvAvruz7yRQXfz2]{Long Jumps} decrease by 10. This doesn't combine with other abilities that reduce those DCs.
  • The distance you can move with a vertical @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.d5I6018Mci2SWokk]{Leap} increases to 5 feet. Your distance for a horizontal Leap increases to 15 feet if your Speed is at least 15 feet, or to 20 feet if your Speed is at least 30 feet.
