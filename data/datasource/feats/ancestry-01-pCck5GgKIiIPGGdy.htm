Name: Veil May Nom: Vierge voilée État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Born of a blood hag, a fiery blood drinker hidden behind a false skin, you have one blood-red eye and the ability to slough off your appearance in an instant.

You gain the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.Dvz54d6aPhjsmUux]{Lie to Me} skill feat, and you can shed a disguise quickly by peeling off the outer layer of your skin.

Changing from a disguise used to @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.AJstokjdG6iDjVjE]{Impersonate} back to your true appearance is a 3-action activity for you; if you're an expert in Deception, it's a 2-action activity, and if you're legendary in Deception, it takes only a single action. This doesn't allow you to don or remove armor or other equipment any faster, but your physical appearance otherwise returns to its normal state.
