Name: Hook 'Em Nom: Attrapez les État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Requirements A rope is within your reach, or you have a rope or grappling weapon in your hand.

The Salt Breakers learned to use whatever they could during the Vidric Revolution, and they quickly mastered the art of making a rope into a useful tool in combat. You take hold of a nearby rope, throw a rope from your hand, or fire a grappling weapon at a solid surface—and then swing up to twice your Speed. If you end your movement next to an enemy, attempt an Athletics check to @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.PMbdMWc2QroouFGD]{Grapple} that enemy, tying them up in your rope.

Accès Membre des Agitateurs au rang de seconde marque ou supérieur

Conditions Vous disposez d'une corde dans votre allonge ou vous avez déjà en main une corde ou une arme avec un grappin.
