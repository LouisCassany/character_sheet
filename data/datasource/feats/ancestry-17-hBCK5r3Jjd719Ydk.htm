Death sends a soul into the River of Souls. Since your soul has experienced not only death but also rebirth, you can shift your physical body into the Ethereal Plane for brief intervals. You gain @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.D2nPKbIS67m9199U]{Ethereal Jaunt} as a 9th-rank occult innate spell that you can cast once per day.
