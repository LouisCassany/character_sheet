Name: Divine Evolution Nom: Évolution divine État: officielle -- Desc (en) --

The divine might provided by your bloodline flows through you. You gain an additional spell slot of your highest level, which you can use only to cast your choice of @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.rfZpqmj0AIIdkVIs]{Heal} or @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.wdA52JJnsuQWeyqz]{Harm}. You can cast either of these spells using that spell slot, even if they aren't in your spell repertoire.
