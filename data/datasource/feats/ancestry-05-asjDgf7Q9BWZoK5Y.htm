Name: I've Had Many Jobs Nom: J'ai eu de nombreux emplois État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Finding and excelling at odd jobs is child's play to one who has worked many occupations during many lives. When you attempt to @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.QyzlsLrqM0EEwd7j]{Earn Income}, you function as if you were trained in the skill used.
