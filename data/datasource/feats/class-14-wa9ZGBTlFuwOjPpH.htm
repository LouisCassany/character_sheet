Name: Rites Of Transfiguration Nom: Rites de transfiguration État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You can adapt your patron's power to transform other creatures into forms more befitting their behavior or your whims. Your familiar learns @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.dN8QBNuTiaBHCKUe]{Cursed Metamorphosis}, even if cursed metamorphosis isn't on your tradition's spell list. By spending 10 minutes in communion with your familiar, you can replace one spell of 6th-rank or higher you've prepared in one of your witch spell slots with a cursed metamorphosis spell of the same rank.
