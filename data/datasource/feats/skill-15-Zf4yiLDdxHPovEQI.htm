Name: Legendary Codebreaker Nom: Cryptanalyste légendaire État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Your skill with languages and codes is so great that you can decipher information with little more than a quick read through a text. You can @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.d9gbpiQjChYDYA2L]{Decipher Writing} using Society while reading at normal speed. If you slow down and spend the full amount of time that's ordinarily required and roll a success, you get a critical success; if you critically succeed while spending the normal amount of time, you gain a nearly word-for-word understanding of the document.
