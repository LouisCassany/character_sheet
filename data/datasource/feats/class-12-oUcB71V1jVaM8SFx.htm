Name: Shared Assault Nom: Assaut partagé État: libre -- Desc (en) --

In the triumph of battle, you can share the glory with another ally. When the ally you chose for @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.Asb0UsQqeATsxqFJ]{Courageous Assault} critically succeeds at the Strike granted by that action, another ally affected by your @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.IAjvwqgiDr3qGYxY]{Courageous Anthem} composition spell can immediately use a reaction to make a melee Strike. This effect doesn't continue to a third ally, even if the second ally also critically succeeds.
