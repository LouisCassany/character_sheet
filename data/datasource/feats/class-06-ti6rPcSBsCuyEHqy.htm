A vortex of sand and dust surrounds you. Each creature inside your kinetic aura (including you) is @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.DmAIPqOBomZ7H95W]{Concealed} from any creature outside your kinetic aura, and creatures outside the aura are concealed from creatures inside the aura other than you.
