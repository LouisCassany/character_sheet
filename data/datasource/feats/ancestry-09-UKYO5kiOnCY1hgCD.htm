Name: Fiendish Magic Nom: Magie des fiélons État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You possess fiendish magic. Choose two of the following spells: @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.PRrZ7anETWPm90YY]{Disguise Magic}, @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.8ViwItUgwT4lOvvb]{False Vitality}, @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.XXqE1eY3w3z6xJCB]{Invisibility}, @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.jwK43yKsHTkJQvQ9]{See the Unseen}, @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.1xbFBQDRs0hT5xZ9]{Shatter}, or @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.Mkbq9xlAUxHUHyR2]{Paranoia}. You can use each of the chosen spells once per day as 2nd-rank divine innate spells.

Grimspawn typically take false vitality and see the unseen, pitborn typically take paranoia and shatter, and hellspawn typically take invisibility and disguise.
