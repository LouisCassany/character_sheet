Name: Rock Rampart Nom: Rempart de roche État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Drawing on your connection to the Plane of Earth, you form a wall of rock and earth. This has the effect of @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.kOa055FIrO9Smnya]{Wall of Stone}, but the wall's maximum length is 40 feet. The wall lasts until the end of your next turn, but you can Sustain it up to 1 minute.

Level (+4) The maximum length of the wall increases by 10 feet, and the Hit Points of each section increase by 5.
