Name: Pickpocket Nom: Vol à la tire État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You can @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.RDXXE7wMrSPCLv5k]{Steal} or @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.ijZ0DDFpMkWqaShd]{Palm an Object} that's closely guarded, such as in a pocket, without taking the –5 penalty. You can't steal objects that would be extremely noticeable or time consuming to remove (like worn shoes or armor or actively wielded objects). If you're a master in Thievery, you can attempt to Steal from a creature in combat or otherwise on guard. When doing so, Stealing requires 2 actions instead of 1, and you take a –5 penalty.
