Name: Divine Guidance Nom: Assistance divine État: officielle -- Desc (en) --

You're so immersed in divine scripture that you find meaning and guidance for any situation in your texts. Spend 10 minutes @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.d9gbpiQjChYDYA2L]{Deciphering Writing} on religious scriptures of your deity or philosophy while thinking about a particular problem or conundrum you face, and then attempt a Religion check (DC determined by the GM).
