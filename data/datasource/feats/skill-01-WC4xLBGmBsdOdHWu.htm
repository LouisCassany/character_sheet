Name: Natural Medicine Nom: Médecine naturelle État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You can apply natural cures to heal your allies. You can use Nature instead of Medicine to @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.1kGNdIIhuglAjIp9]{Treat Wounds}, including higher Nature proficiency letting you attempt more difficult checks. It doesn't replace Medicine for uses of the skill other than Treat Wounds or for feat prerequisites.
