Name: Dragon Shape Nom: Morphologie draconique État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You can take on the shape of some of the world's most fearsome creatures. Add the shapes listed in @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.5c692cCcTDXjSEzk]{Dragon Form} to your @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.8RWfKConLYFZpQ9X]{Untamed Form} list. Whenever you're polymorphed into another shape using untamed form, you gain resistance 5 to your choice of acid, cold, electricity, fire, or poison.
