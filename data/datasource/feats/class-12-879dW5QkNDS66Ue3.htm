Razor-sharp slivers of metal fall from the sky. Each creature in a @Template[burst|distance:20] within 60 feet takes @Damage[(floor((@actor.level -12)/2)+9)d6[slashing]|traits:area-damage] damage with a @Check[reflex|against:kineticist|basic] save against your class DC. The razors embed in all surfaces in the area, making them hazardous terrain for 1 minute. A creature that moves through this hazardous terrain takes @Damage[(floor((max(12,@actor.level)-12)/2)+3)[slashing]] damage for every square of the area it moves into.

Level (+2) The initial damage increases by 1d6 and the hazardous terrain damage increases by 1.
