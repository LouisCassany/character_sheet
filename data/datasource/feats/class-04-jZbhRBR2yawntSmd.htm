Name: Agile Hand Nom: Manipulation agile État: libre -- Desc (en) --

When you cast @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.pwzdSlJgYqN7bs2w]{Telekinetic Hand}, you can modify its target to be a @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.equipment-srd.Item.zvLyCVD8g2PdHJAc]{Thieves' Toolkit}. When you Cast the Spell in this way, the tools move up to 20 feet towards a device or lock. After you Sustain the spell on future turns, you can use the tools to attempt Thievery checks to @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.cYdz2grcOcRt4jk6]{Disable a Device} or @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.2EE4aF4SZpYf0R6H]{Pick a Lock} within the spell's range, but you take a -2 penalty to your Thievery check. If you critically fail, the spell ends and you can't use this modification again for 24 hours.
