Name: Fade Away Nom: Disparition État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Your ability to blend into the background allows you to fade away entirely or appear innocuous even to magical effects. You gain @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.XXqE1eY3w3z6xJCB]{Invisibility} and @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.PRrZ7anETWPm90YY]{Disguise Magic} as 2nd-rank innate occult spells. You can target only yourself with invisibility, and you must be the primary target of misdirection. You can cast each spell once per day.
