Name: Eclectic Polymath Nom: Touche-à-tout éclectique État: officielle -- Desc (en) --

Your flexible mind can quickly shift from one spell to another. If you add a spell to your repertoire during your daily preparations using @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.4HZTLPKPteEFsa7n]{Esoteric Polymath}, when you prepare again, you can choose to keep the new spell from Esoteric Polymath in your repertoire and instead lose access to another spell of the same rank in your repertoire.

-- Desc (fr) --

Votre esprit flexible peut rapidement échanger un sort contre un autre.
