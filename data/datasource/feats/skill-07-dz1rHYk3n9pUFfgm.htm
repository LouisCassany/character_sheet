Name: Bless Toxin Nom: Toxine bénie État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You pour void energy into toxins you create to further debilitate your targets. When you @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.rmwa3OyhTZ2i2AHl]{Craft} an alchemical poison (rather than create it with advanced alchemy, Quick Alchemy, or another means), it becomes a blessed alchemical poison and gains the void trait. When a creature is afflicted with a blessed alchemical poison, for 1 round, any Hit Points it would regain from a vitality effect are reduced by 5, or by 10 if you are legendary in Crafting.
