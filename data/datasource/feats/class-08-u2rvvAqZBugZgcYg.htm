The extent of your corruption devastates your foes' minds and reflexes. When you use @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.vxA0VRN10OwUkGAr]{Cruelty}, you can choose to make the target @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.i3OJZU2nk64Df3xm]{Clumsy} or @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.e1XGnhKNSQIm5IXg]{Stupefied} instead, with the same condition value.
