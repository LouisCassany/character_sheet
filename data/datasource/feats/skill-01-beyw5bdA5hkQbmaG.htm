Name: Terrain Stalker Nom: Traqueur de terrain État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Select one type of difficult terrain from the following list: rubble, snow, or underbrush. While @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.VRSef5y1LmL2Hkjf]{Undetected} by all non-allies in that type of terrain, you can @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.VMozDqMMuK5kpoX4]{Sneak} without attempting a Stealth check as long as you move no more than 5 feet and do not pass within 10 feet of an enemy during your movement.

During exploration, this also allows you to automatically approach within 15 feet of other creatures while @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.IE2nThCmoyhQA0Jn]{Avoiding their Notice}, as long as they aren't actively @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.TiNDYUGlMmxzxBYU]{Searching} or on guard.

Special You can select this feat multiple times. Each time, choose a different type of terrain.
