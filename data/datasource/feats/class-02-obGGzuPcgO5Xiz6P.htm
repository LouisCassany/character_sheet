Name: Searing Restoration Nom: Guérison explosive État: changé Changés: desc État d'origine: libre -- Desc (en) --

They told you there was no way that explosions could heal people, but they were fools… Fools who didn't understand your brilliance! You create a minor explosion from your innovation, altering the combustion to cauterize wounds using vaporized medicinal herbs. You or a living creature adjacent to you regains @Damage[max(1,ceil(@actor.level/2))d10[healing]] Hit Points. In addition, the creature you heal can attempt an immediate flat check to recover from a single source of persistent bleed damage, with the DC reduction from appropriate assistance. At 3rd level, and every 2 levels thereafter, increase the healing by 1d10.

Special If your innovation is a minion, it can take this action rather than you, though because it's not a living creature, it can't use the ability on itself.

Ils vous ont affirmé que des explosions qui pourraient guérir les gens étaient impossibles, mais ce sont des imbéciles... Des imbéciles qui n'ont pas compris votre génie ! Vous créez une explosion mineure à partir de votre innovation, modifiant la combustion pour cautériser les blessures en utilisant des plantes médicinales vaporisées. Vous ou une créature vivante adjacente à vous regagnez @Damage[max(1,ceil(@actor.level/2))d10[healing]]{1d10 points de vie}.
