Name: Dread Marshal Stance Nom: Posture du terrible Capitaine État: changé Changés: desc État d'origine: libre -- Desc (en) --

Putting on a grim face for the battle ahead, you encourage your allies to strike fear into their foes with vicious attacks. When you use this action, attempt an @Check[intimidation|dc:@self.level|adjustment:-2|roller:self] check. The DC is usually an easy DC of your level, but the GM can assign a different DC based on the circumstances. The effect depends on the result of your check.

@UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feat-effects.Item.qX62wJzDYtNxDbFv]{Stance: Dread Marshal Stance}

Success Your marshal's aura grants you and your allies in the aura a +1 status bonus to damage rolls. When you or an ally in the aura critically hit an enemy with a Strike, that enemy is @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.TBSHQspnbcqxsmjL]{Frightened 1}. If you're wielding a weapon that has more than one damage die (typically due to a striking rune), you can have the status bonus equal the weapon's number of damage dice instead of +1.

Failure You fail to enter the stance.

Critical Failure You fail to enter the stance and can't take this action again for 1 minute.
