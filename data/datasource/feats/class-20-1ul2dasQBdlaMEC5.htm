Name: Heart of the Kaiju Nom: Coeur de kaiju État: changé Changés: desc État d'origine: libre -- Desc (en) --

Witnessing the might of the kaiju King Mogaru, Alru, and Toraloa up close awoke a new ferocity within you. When you transform using @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.54JzsYCx3uoj7Wlz]{Monstrosity Shape}, you can take on a kaiju form. When you take this feat, choose acid, cold, electricity, fire, or sonic damage; this is the damage your breath weapon deals. You can't change this later. The battle form is Gargantuan size with the following specific abilities; the damage values already include the extra die from being a 9th-rank spell.

Kaiju Speed 40 feet; physical resistance 5;

Melee 1 jaws (reach 20 feet), Damage 3d12+20 piercing;

Melee 1 claws (agile, reach 20 feet), Damage 3d8+20 slashing;

Breath Weapon 2 (evocation, primal) Each creature in a @Template[cone|distance:60] takes @Damage[15d6[@item.flags.pf2e.rulesSelections.damageType]] damage (of your chosen damage type), with a basic @Check[reflex|against:spell|basic] save against your spell DC. Once activated, your breath weapon can't be used again for [[/r 1d4 #Recharge Breath Weapon]]{1d4 rounds}.
