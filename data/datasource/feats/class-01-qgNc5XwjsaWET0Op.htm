You apply your flashy techniques to thrown weapons as easily as melee attacks. You apply your @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.classfeatures.Item.RQH6vigvhmiYKKjg]{Precise Strike} damage on ranged Strikes you make with a thrown weapon within that weapon's first range increment. The thrown weapon must be an agile or finesse weapon. This also allows you to make a thrown weapon ranged Strike for @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.K878asDgf1EF0B9S]{Confident Finisher} and any other finisher that includes a Strike that can benefit from your precise strike.
