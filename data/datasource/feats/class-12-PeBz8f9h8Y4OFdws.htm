Name: Legs of Stone Nom: Jambes de pierre État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You gain a +2 status bonus to your Fortitude and Reflex DCs against attempts to @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.7blmbDrQFNfdT731]{Shove} or @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.ge56Lu1xXVFYUnLP]{Trip} you. When you successfully Shove a foe, you can Stride away from your opponent (instead of toward it), but you must move the same distance in the opposite direction from where you Shoved it.
