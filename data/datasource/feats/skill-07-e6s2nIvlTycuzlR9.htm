Name: Fabricated Connections Nom: Réalisations inventées État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You can @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.ewwCglB7XOPLUz72]{Lie} about having taken an action so convincingly that you acquire all the tangible benefits of having successfully completed it.

You can roll Deception in place of a different skill when attempting to @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.QyzlsLrqM0EEwd7j]{Earn Income}, @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.OX4fy22hQgUHDr0q]{Make an Impression}, @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.DCb62iCBrJXy0Ik6]{Request}, or @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.49y9Ec4bDii8pcD3]{Subsist}.

You can use Fabricated Connections to Make an Impression or Request once per day, and you can also use it once per week to Earn Income or Subsist.
