Name: Overextending Feint Nom: Feinte précautionneuse État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You goad a foe into overextending. On a successful @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.QNAVeNKtHA0EUw4X]{Feint}, you can use the following success and critical success effects instead of any other effects that would occur when you Feint.

Critical Success The target takes a –2 circumstance penalty to all attack rolls against you before the end of its next turn.

Success The target takes a –2 circumstance penalty to its next attack roll against you before the end of its next turn.
