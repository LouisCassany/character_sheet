Trigger You're the target of a physical attack.

You grow a shield of woven flowers and branches, then Raise that Shield to defend yourself from the triggering attack. If you would take damage from the attack, you immediately @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.jM72TjJ965jocBV8]{Shield Block}. This is a @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.equipment-srd.Item.ezVp13Uw8cWW08Da]{Wooden Shield} (Hardness 3, HP 12, BT 6). The shield remains in your possession for a number of rounds equal to your level, or until it's destroyed.

Awakening The shield becomes stronger. It has Hardness 5, HP 40, BT 20.

Awakening The shield takes on a mind of its own and lashes out vengefully when destroyed. When the shield is destroyed, is explodes, dealing @Damage[4d6[piercing]] damage to your attacker, with a @Check[reflex|against:class-spell|basic] save.
