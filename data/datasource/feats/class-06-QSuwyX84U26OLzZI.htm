Name: Predictive Purchase (Investigator) Nom: Achat intuitif (Enquêteur) État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You have just the thing for the situation. You gain the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.bh2jHyyYrkLMsIdX]{Prescient Planner} and @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.lHFz4MmebvPqTb0A]{Prescient Consumable} feats.
