Name: Glamour Nom: Enchantement État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You unlock a new level of fey power and can manipulate the perceptions of others using a font of First World magic. You gain the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.DdXKfIjDtORUtUvY]{Fey Glamour} focus spell as a primal spell that doesn't have the sorcerer trait.
