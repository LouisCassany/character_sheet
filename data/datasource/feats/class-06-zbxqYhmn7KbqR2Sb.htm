Name: Shatter Defenses Nom: Briser les défenses État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Requirements A frightened creature is in your melee reach.

Your offense exploits your enemy's fear. Make a melee Strike against a frightened creature. If you hit and deal damage, the target becomes @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.AJh5ex99aV6VTggg]{Off-Guard} until its frightened condition ends. If the target was already off-guard to you when you damaged it with this Strike, it can't reduce its frightened value below 1 until the start of your next turn.
