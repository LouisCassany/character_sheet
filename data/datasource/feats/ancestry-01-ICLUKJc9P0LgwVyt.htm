Name: Brightsoul Nom: Âme-éclat État: libre -- Desc (en) --

The fire inside you asserts itself as beaming, radiant light. Your body is naturally luminescent, glowing with the effects of a primal @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.WBmvzNDfpwka3qT4]{Light} cantrip. A cantrip is heightened to a spell rank equal to half your level rounded up. The light is involuntary and constant; if counteracted, it returns in [[/br 1d4 #rounds]]{1d4 rounds}. You suffer a -2 circumstance penalty on Stealth checks to Hide or Sneak and gain a +1 circumstance bonus on saves against light effects and effects that inflict the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.XgEqL1kFApUbl5Z2]{Blinded} or @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.TkIyaNPgTZFBCCuh]{Dazzled} conditions.
