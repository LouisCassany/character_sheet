Name: Sacred Defense Nom: Défense sacrée État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Frequency once per hour

You call upon your deity to grant you their protective blessing. Attempt a @Check[religion|dc:30] check. If you have legendary proficiency in Religion, you can instead attempt a @Check[religion|dc:40] check to increase the temporary Hit Points you gain on a successful check to 15 (25 on a critical success).

Critical Success You gain 10 temporary Hit Points. You lose any remaining temporary Hit Points after 1 minute.

Success You gain 5 temporary Hit Points. You lose any remaining temporary Hit Points after 1 minute.

Critical Failure Your deity withholds their blessing. You cannot attempt to call to your deity again for 1 day.
