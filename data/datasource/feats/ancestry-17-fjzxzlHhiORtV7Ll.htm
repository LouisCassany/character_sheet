Name: Boneyard Acquaintance Nom: Fréquentation du Cimetière État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Before your soul returned to the Material Plane, you interacted with a host of other souls on their way to the Boneyard. One of these acquaintances owes you a favor. You can temporarily summon it to come to your aid, and it appears as a swirling mass of ectoplasm with indiscernible features and a translucent @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.equipment-srd.Item.rQWaJhI5Bko5x14Z]{Dagger}, the favored weapon of Pharasma. This functions as @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.jQdm301h6e8hIY4U]{Spiritual Guardian}, which you can cast once per day as a divine innate spell, but you don't need to worship a deity to do so.
