Name: Mature Megafauna Companion Nom: Compagnon mégafaune adulte État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You've raised your megafauna companion to be a more powerful force on the battlefield than most of its kind. The companion you gained through the Mammoth Lord Dedication feat becomes a mature animal companion, granting it additional capabilities. During an encounter, even if you don't use the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.q9nbyIF0PEBqMtYe]{Command an Animal} action, your megafauna animal companion can still use 1 action on your turn to Stride or Strike.
