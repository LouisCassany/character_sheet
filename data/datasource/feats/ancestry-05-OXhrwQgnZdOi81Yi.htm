Name: Defy Death Nom: Défier la mort État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You're exceptionally difficult to kill. The DC of your recovery checks is equal to 9 + your dying value, or 8 + your dying value if you have the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.AmP0qu7c5dlBSath]{Toughness} general feat. In addition, whenever someone returns you to life using magic that would normally leave you debilitated for a week (such as @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.IkGYwHRLhkuoGReG]{Raise Dead} or the resurrect ritual), you don't suffer that condition.
