Name: Barrier Shield Nom: Bouclier barrière État: libre -- Desc (en) --

When you cast @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.TVKNbcgTee19PXZR]{Shield}, you can modify the spell to create a solid barrier you can use for @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.other-effects.Item.I9lfZUiCwMiGogVi]{Cover}, but not for blocking. You can't use the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.jM72TjJ965jocBV8]{Shield Block} reaction when the spell is modified in this way, but you (and only you) can spend an action to @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.ust1jJSCZQUhBZIz]{Take Cover} to gain standard cover from it.
