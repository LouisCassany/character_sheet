Name: Lie to Me Nom: Ose me mentir État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You can use Deception to weave traps to trip up anyone trying to deceive you. If you can engage in conversation with someone trying to @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.ewwCglB7XOPLUz72]{Lie} to you, use your Deception DC if it is higher than your Perception DC to determine whether they succeed. This doesn't apply if you don't have a back-and-forth dialogue, such as when someone attempts to Lie during a long speech.
