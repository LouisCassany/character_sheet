You have tattoos on your body corresponding to one of the ancient Thassilonian schools of magic. Choose one of the following schools of magic: abjuration (@UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.TVKNbcgTee19PXZR]{Shield}), conjuration (@UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.uZK2BYzPnxUBnDjr]{Tangle Vine}), enchantment (@UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.4gBIw4IDrSfFHik4]{Daze}), evocation (@UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.kBhaPuzLUSwS6vVf]{Electric Arc}), illusion (@UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.atlgGNI1E1Ox3O3a]{Ghost Sound}), necromancy (@UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.mAMEt4FFbdqoRnkN]{Void Warp}), or transmutation (@UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.AUctDF2fqPZN2w4W]{Sigil}). You can cast the associated cantrip (listed in parentheses) as an innate arcane spell at will.
