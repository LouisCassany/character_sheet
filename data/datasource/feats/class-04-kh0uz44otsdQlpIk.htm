Name: Calcifying Sand Nom: Sable calcifiant État: changé Changés: desc État d'origine: libre -- Desc (en) --

Trigger A creature damages you with an attack using an unarmed attack or a non-reach melee weapon.

Your flesh gives way as you're struck, becoming coarse sand that can magically turn your enemy to stone. You gain resistance equal to your level to physical damage from the triggering attack. The attacking creature must attempt a @Check[fortitude|against:class] save against your class DC. Regardless of the result, that creature can't trigger Calcifying Sand again for 1 hour.

Success The creature is unaffected.

Failure The creature is @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.xYTAsEpcJE1Ccni3]{Slowed 1} until the end of its next turn.

Critical Failure The creature is @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.dTwPJuKgBQCMxixg]{Petrified} until the end of its next turn.

Succès La créature n'est pas affectée.

Échec La créature est @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.xYTAsEpcJE1Ccni3]{Ralentie 1} jusqu'à la fin de son prochain tour.

Échec critique La créature est @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.dTwPJuKgBQCMxixg]{Pétrifiée} jusqu'à la fin de son prochain tour.
