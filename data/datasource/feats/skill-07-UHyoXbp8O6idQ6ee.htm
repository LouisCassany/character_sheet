Name: Bless Tonic Nom: Tonique béni État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You channel vitality energy into restoratives that ward against harm. When you @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.rmwa3OyhTZ2i2AHl]{Craft} an alchemical item that restores Hit Points (rather than create it with advanced alchemy, Quick Alchemy, or another means), it becomes a blessed alchemical item and gains the vitality trait. For example, an @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.equipment-srd.Item.hDLbR56Id2OtU318]{Elixir of Life} would become a blessed elixir of life. When a creature regains Hit Points using a blessed alchemical item, the overflowing energy grants them void resistance 5 for 1 round, or void resistance 10 if you are legendary in Crafting.
