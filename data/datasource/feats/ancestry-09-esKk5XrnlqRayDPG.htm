You possess celestial magic. Choose two of the following spells: @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.EfFMLVbmkBWmzoLF]{Clear Mind}, @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.ynm8JIU3sc3qUMpa]{Everlight}, @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.2qGqa33E4GPUCbMV]{Humanoid Form}, @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.0qaqksrGGDj74HXE]{Revealing Light}, @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.d7Lwx6KAs47MtF0q]{Share Life}, or @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.BCuHKrDeJ4eq53M6]{Sure Footing}.
