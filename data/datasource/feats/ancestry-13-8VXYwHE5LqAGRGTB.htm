Name: Kashrishi Revivification Nom: Revitalisation kashrishie État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Frequency once per day

Trigger You have the dying condition and are about to attempt a recovery check. You can use Kashrishi Revivification even if you're otherwise unable to act.

The well of psychic energy held within your mind can exceed the limitations of your physical body. You're restored to 1 Hit Point, lose the dying and @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.fBnFDH2MTzgFijKf]{Unconscious} conditions, and can act normally on this turn. You gain or increase the wounded condition as normal for losing the dying condition.
