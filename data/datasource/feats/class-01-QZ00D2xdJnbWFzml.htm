Name: Blowgun Poisoner Nom: Libération subtile État: changé Changés: desc,name,rules.0.text État d'origine: libre -- Desc (en) --

You can capably deliver toxins with a @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.equipment-srd.Item.FPwsiGqMCNPLHmjX]{Blowgun}. Your blowgun @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.VjxZFuUXrCU94MWR]{Strikes} can apply injury poisons even if they deal no damage due to a creature's resistance. If you critically succeed at a blowgun Strike using a poisoned dart, the target's initial save against the poison is one degree of success worse than the creature rolls; this is a misfortune effect.

In addition, if you make a blowgun Strike while @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.iU0fEDdBp3rXpTMC]{Hidden} or @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.VRSef5y1LmL2Hkjf]{Undetected}, you don't automatically become @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.1wQY3JYyhMYeeV2G]{Observed}. Instead, immediately attempt a @Check[stealth] check against the Perception DC of the target. If you succeed, you don't become observed, and are hidden (if you were undetected, you still become hidden rather than remaining undetected).
