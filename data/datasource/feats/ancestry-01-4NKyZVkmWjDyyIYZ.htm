Name: Elven Weapon Familiarity Nom: Familiarité avec les armes elfiques État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You favor bows and other elegant weapons. You gain access to all uncommon weapons with the elf trait. You have familiarity with weapons with the elf trait plus @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.equipment-srd.Item.MVAWttmT0QDa7LsV]{Longbows}, @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.equipment-srd.Item.dUC8Fsa6FZtVikS3]{Composite Longbows}, @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.equipment-srd.Item.tH5GirEy7YB3ZgCk]{Rapiers}, @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.equipment-srd.Item.hIgqLgH3YcLZBeoT]{Shortbows}, and @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.equipment-srd.Item.e4NwsnPnpQKbDZ9F]{Composite Shortbows}—for the purposes of proficiency, you treat any of these that are martial weapons as simple weapons and any that are advanced weapons as martial weapons.

At 5th level, whenever you get a critical hit with one of these weapons, you get its critical specialization effect.
