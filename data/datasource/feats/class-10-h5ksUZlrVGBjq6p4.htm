Your blessed armament radiates power, further enhancing your chosen weapon. When you choose the weapon for your blessed armament during your daily preparations, add the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.equipment-srd.Item.6llILJtsTPgtYXgx]{Astral} and @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.equipment-srd.Item.LbdnZFlyLFdAE617]{Brilliant} property runes to the list of effects you can choose from. If you're holy, also add the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.equipment-srd.Item.DH0kB9Wbr5pDeunX]{Holy} rune, and if you're unholy, also add the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.equipment-srd.Item.gmMrJREf4JSHd2dZ]{Unholy} rune.

In addition, you can change the rune you've selected for the day to a different rune from your list as a 10-minute activity that has the concentrate, divine, and exploration traits. Changing the rune doesn't restore abilities that can be used only a limited number of times, such as Holy Healing for the holy rune
