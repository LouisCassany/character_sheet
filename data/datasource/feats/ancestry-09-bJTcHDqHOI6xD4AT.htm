Name: Unyielding Disguise Nom: Déguisement infaillible État: libre -- Desc (en) --

When you cast @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.2qGqa33E4GPUCbMV]{Humanoid Form} or @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.i35dpZFI7jZcRoBo]{Illusory Disguise} to alter your appearance, the spell's duration is 2 hours. At the end of the duration, you can extend the spell's duration for another hour, but doing so is taxing, making you @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.4D2KBtexWXa6oUMR]{Drained 1}. You can keep extending it in this way, increasing your drained condition by 1 each time, to a maximum duration of 6 hours.
