Name: Friendly Nudge Nom: Coup de pouce amical État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You don't always have time to wait for your allies to make room, so it's best to politely move them yourself. Attempt an Athletics check to @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.7blmbDrQFNfdT731]{Shove} an ally. You don't need to have a free hand to attempt this check. If your ally is willing, treat the result as one degree of success higher.
