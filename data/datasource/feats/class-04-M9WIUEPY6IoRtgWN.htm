Name: Gravelands Herbalist Nom: Herboriste des Terres tombales État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Access Knights of Lastwall have access to this feat.

Your time with the Knights of Lastwall has taught you the secrets of creating and improving upon @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.equipment-srd.Item.VeCNWhvEr82ZNoSV]{Lastwall Soup}. Add Lastwall soup to your list of herbal items. At 10th level, you can make @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.equipment-srd.Item.VcEd9Bh4q5vQfpLv]{Lastwall Soup (Improved)} that grants a +3 item bonus. At 18th level, you can make @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.equipment-srd.Item.ed8gAxli6Of58kAM]{Lastwall Soup (Greater)} that grants a +4 item bonus, and its nourishing properties grant you 10 temporary Hit Points that last up to 10 minutes after eating it.
