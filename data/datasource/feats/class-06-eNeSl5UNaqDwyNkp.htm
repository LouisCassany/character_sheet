Name: Eidolon's Wrath Nom: Courroux de l'eidolon État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Your eidolon gains the eidolon's wrath focus spell, which it casts, instead of you. You determine the damage type when you gain the feat: acid, cold, electricity, fire, sonic, vitality, or void. If your eidolon is a celestial, fiend, or monitor with an alignment other than true neutral, you can choose a damage type in its alignment.

Increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1. As normal, your eidolon shares your Focus Points.
