You can create endless shorter-lived snares, protecting your lair or using them against foes.

Once per minute, you can @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.rmwa3OyhTZ2i2AHl]{Craft} one of the snares you've prepared for quick deployment without expending the prepared snare. Once deployed, such a snare lasts for 10 minutes before losing its effectiveness.
