Name: Stance: Reflective Ripple Stance Nom: Effet : Posture de l'onde réfléchissante État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You can make flowing wave attacks that deal 1d6 bludgeoning damage. They are in the brawling group and have the agile, disarm, finesse, nonlethal, trip, unarmed, and water traits.

While in Reflective Ripple Stance, you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to Athletics checks to @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.Dt6B1slsBy8ipJu9]{Disarm}, @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.c8TGiZ48ygoSPofx]{Swim}, or @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.ge56Lu1xXVFYUnLP]{Trip}, and you gain a +2 circumstance bonus to your Reflex DC to avoid being Disarmed and Tripped.
