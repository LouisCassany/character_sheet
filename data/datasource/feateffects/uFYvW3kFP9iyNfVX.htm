Granted by @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.uJXs4M6IeixfPBLc]{Clinging Shadows Stance}

You adopt the stance of clinging shadows. You can make shadow grasp Strikes. These deal 1d4 void damage; are in the brawling group; and have the agile, grapple, reach, and unarmed traits.

While in the clinging shadows stance, you gain a +2 circumstance bonus to Athletics checks to @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.PMbdMWc2QroouFGD]{Grapple}, and to the DC for creatures to @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.SkZAQRkLLkmBQNB9]{Escape} from you.
