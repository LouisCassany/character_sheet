Name: Dragon Breath Nom: Souffle de dragon État: changé Changés: desc État d'origine: officielle -- Desc (en) --

You spew energy from your mouth, dealing 5d6 damage with a basic Reflex save. The damage type depends on the magical tradition related to the dragon that influenced your bloodline.

Heightened (+1) The damage increases by 2d6.
