If you hit, you deal 4d6 bludgeoning damage plus 1d6 bludgeoning splash damage. On a critical hit, double the initial damage, but not the splash damage, and the target you hit must succeed at a Fortitude saving throw or fall @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.j91X7x0XSomq8d60]{Prone}.
