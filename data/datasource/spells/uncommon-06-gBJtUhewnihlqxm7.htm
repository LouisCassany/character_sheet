Name: Rose's Thorns Nom: Épines de rose État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Rose bushes with razor-sharp thorns sprout from the ground in the area. The rose bushes deal @Damage[8d8[piercing]] damage to each creature that's in the area when the spell is cast or that ends its turn in the area (basic Reflex save). A creature that critically fails its saving throw also takes @Damage[1d8[persistent,bleed]] damage and is @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.eIcWbB5o3pP6OIMe]{Immobilized} for 1 round or until the creature @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.SkZAQRkLLkmBQNB9]{Escapes} (against your spell DC), whichever comes first. The area is difficult terrain and hazardous terrain. A creature that moves through the area takes @Damage[5[piercing]] for every square of that area it moves into. Each time you sustain this spell, the radius of the burst increases by 5 feet.

Heightened (9th) The initial damage increases to @Damage[12d8[piercing]], the persistent bleed damage increases to @Damage[2d8[persistent,bleed]], and the hazardous terrain damage increases to @Damage[8[piercing]].
