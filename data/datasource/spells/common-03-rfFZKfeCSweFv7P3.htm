Name: Impending Doom Nom: Malheur annoncé État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You sift through myriad potential futures, seize upon one potential moment in which the target meets a particularly gruesome and fatal end, and then show it a vision of its impending demise. The intense mental vision grows more and more terrifying to the target as the doom grows closer, and it takes 3 rounds to reach its conclusion. The target must attempt a Will saving throw to determine the effects. At the end of the spell's duration, if the target was affected, the target witnesses its death and takes 6d6 mental damage.

Critical Success The creature is unaffected.

Success The creature is unaffected for 1 round. On the second round, it becomes @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.AJh5ex99aV6VTggg]{Off-Guard}. Finally, on the third round, it becomes @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.TBSHQspnbcqxsmjL]{Frightened 1}. At the end of the third round, it takes half damage.

Failure The creature is immediately off-guard. On the second round, it becomes @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.TBSHQspnbcqxsmjL]{Frightened 2}. Finally, on the third round, it also becomes @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.dfCMdR4wnpbYNTix]{Stunned 1}. At the end of the third round, the creature takes full damage.

Critical Failure The creature is immediately off-guard and @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.TBSHQspnbcqxsmjL]{Frightened 3}. On the second round, it becomes stunned 1. Finally, on the third round, it also becomes @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.6uEgoh53GbXuHpTF]{Paralyzed}. At the end of the third round, the creature takes double damage.

Heightened (+1) The damage increases by 2d6.

Vous passez en revue une myriade de futurs potentiels, puis saisissez un de ces moments où la cible connaît une fin particulièrement horrible et fatale, puis vous lui montrez une vision de sa mort imminente. La vision mentale intense devient de plus en plus terrifiante pour la cible à mesure que le destin se rapproche et il faut 3 rounds pour qu'elle parvienne à sa conclusion.
