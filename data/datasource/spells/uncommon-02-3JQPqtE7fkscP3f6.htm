Name: Canopy Crawler Nom: Arpenteur de la canopée État: libre -- Desc (en) --

The target grows a prehensile tail, or gains prehensile qualities in their existing tail, to help them climb with ease. For the duration of the spell, they gain a climb Speed equal to their highest Speed. The target can Climb with a hand occupied (or two hands occupied if they have the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.09PurtIanNUPfNRq]{Combat Climber} feat).
