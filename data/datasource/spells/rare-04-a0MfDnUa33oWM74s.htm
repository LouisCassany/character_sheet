Name: Tomorrow's Dawn Nom: Aube de demain État: libre -- Desc (en) --

The golden light of a new dawn and the promise of life beyond death radiates from your body. You cast bright light in a 20-foot radius (and dim light for the next 20 feet) and gain mental resistance 5. Any nindoru fiend or undead that begins its turn adjacent to you must attempt a Will save with the following results.

@UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spell-effects.Item.SyF5kpZlZuBF4lMf]{Spell Effect: Tomorrow's Dawn}

Critical Success The creature is unaffected and immune to this spell's effects for the remainder of the spell's duration.

Success The creature is @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.fesd1n5eVhpCSS18]{Sickened 1} until the end of its turn.

Failure The creature is sickened 1. In addition, it becomes @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.xYTAsEpcJE1Ccni3]{Slowed 1} until the end of its next turn.

Critical Failure As failure, but @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.fesd1n5eVhpCSS18]{Sickened 2} and the creature remains slowed 1 for the rest of the spell's duration.

Heightened (6th) You gain mental resistance 10.

Heightened (8th) You gain mental resistance 15.
