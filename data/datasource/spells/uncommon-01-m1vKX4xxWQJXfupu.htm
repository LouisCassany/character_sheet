Name: Glowing Trail Nom: Trace luisante État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Your movements leave a vague glowing path behind you, like a misty echo of your presence. While your form is not discernible, your size is. This path hangs in space and is not anchored to any surface, so it tracks your movement through the air. The path fades after 10 minutes. You can Dismiss this spell at any time, but the path fades normally. The path can be visible or @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.zJxUflt9np0q4yML]{Invisible}. While visible, it appears as a faintly glowing mist in a color of your choosing but sheds no light beyond its area. While invisible, you can still detect the path, but it gives off no light.

Heightened (3rd) The glowing trail fades after 1 day.

Heightened (5th) The glowing trail fades after 1 week.

Heightened (7th) The glowing trail fades after 1 month.

Heightened (9th) The glowing trail fades after 1 year.

Vos déplacements laissent un vague chemin brillant derrière vous, comme un écho brumux de votre présence. Bien que votre forme ne soit plus discernable, votre taille l'est. S'il est invisible, vous pouvez toujours détecter le chemin mais il ne donne pas de lumière.

Intensifié (5e) La trace brillante disparaît après 1 semaine.
