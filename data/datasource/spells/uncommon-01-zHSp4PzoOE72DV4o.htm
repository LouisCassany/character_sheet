Name: Torturous Trauma Nom: Trauma de la torture État: libre -- Desc (en) --

With this spell born of Kemnebi's sadistic love of torture, you batter a creature's internal organs, leaving no external signs of the immense pain you delivered. The target takes bludgeoning damage equal to 1d4 + your spellcasting modifier with a basic Fortitude save. If it critically fails, the target is also @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.HL2l2VRSaQHu9lUw]{Fatigued} until the start of your next turn. Creatures that lack internal organs are unaffected by this spell.

Heightened (+1) The bludgeoning damage increases by 1d4.
