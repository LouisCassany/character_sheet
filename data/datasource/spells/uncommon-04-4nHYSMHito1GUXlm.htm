Name: Rebuke Death Nom: Réprimander la mort État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You snatch creatures from the jaws of death, restoring them without the straing of a typical close call. You can spend 1 to 3 actions Casting this Spell, and you can target a number of creatures equal to the actions spent. Each target regains 3d6 Hit Points. If the target had the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.yZRUzMqrMmfLu0V1]{Dying} condition, coming back from Dying due to this healing doesn't increase its @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.Yl48xTdMh3aeQYL2]{Wounded} condition.

Heightened (+1) Increase the healing by 1d6.
