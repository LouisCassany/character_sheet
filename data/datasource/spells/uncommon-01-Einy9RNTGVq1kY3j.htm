Name: Inside Ropes Nom: Cordes internes État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You pull a large handful of guts from your midsection. Removing these guts doesn't harm you. You can use the guts as 50 feet of rope to help you @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.pprgrYQ1QnIDGZiy]{Climb}, and they help attach you to a wall. You move half as quickly as usual while climbing with your guts (minimum 5 feet), but you can attempt a @Check[flat|dc:5] check whenever you critically fail to prevent a fall. Other creatures can use your guts to climb like a squishy rope, but the guts don't help anyone but you attach to a wall. If you let go of your guts or your guts are damaged, the spell ends.

Heightened (3rd) You gain a +1 item bonus to Athletics checks to Climb while using your guts.

Vous retirez une bonne poignée de boyaux de votre abdomen. Retirer ces boyaux ne vous blesse en rien. Si vous lâchez vos boyaux ou si vos boyaux sont endommagés, le sort prend fin.

Intensifié (3e) Vous obtenez un bonus d'objet de +1 aux tests d'Athlétisme pour Escalader en utilisant vos entrailles.
