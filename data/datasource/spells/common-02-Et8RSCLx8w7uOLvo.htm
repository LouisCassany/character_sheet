Name: Sound Body Nom: Corps assaini État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You send a surge of healing energy to restore the target's body. Attempt to counteract an effect of your choice imposing one of these conditions on the target: @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.XgEqL1kFApUbl5Z2]{Blinded}, @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.TkIyaNPgTZFBCCuh]{Dazzled}, @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.9PR9y0bi4JPKnHPR]{Deafened}, @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.MIRkyAjyBeXivMa7]{Enfeebled}, or @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.fesd1n5eVhpCSS18]{Sickened}. If you didn't counteract the effect, but you would have if its counteract rank were 2 lower, instead suppress the effect until the beginning of your next turn. This spell can't counteract or suppress curses, diseases, or conditions that are part of the target's normal state.
