Name: Blast of the Bellows Nom: Souffle des soufflets État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You create a churning eddy of warm air that makes it difficult to fly within the area. This area is difficult terrain for flying creatures. Each time a flying creature enters the area or ends its turn there, it must attempt an Acrobatics check to @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.Qf1ylAbdVi1rkc8M]{Maneuver in Flight}.

Success The creature is unaffected.

Failure The creature descends 30 feet.

Critical Failure The creature descends 60 feet.

Heightened (4th) The duration increases to 10 minutes.
