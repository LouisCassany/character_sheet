Name: Songbird's Call Nom: Appel de l'oiseau chanteur État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Your voice calls a storm of songbirds that whirl around you and peck at your enemies. All creatures within the area of whirling birds become @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.DmAIPqOBomZ7H95W]{Concealed}, and all creatures outside the area become concealed to creatures within it. A creature other than you that enters or ends its turn in the area takes 1d6 piercing damage as the birds peck at it.

Heightened (+2) The damage increases by 1d6.
