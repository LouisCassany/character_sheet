Name: Life Connection Nom: Connexion vitale État: libre -- Desc (en) --

When you cast this spell, you place a failsafe deep within the life force of the target. You can sense when the target takes damage, provided you're within 1 mile of one another. A creature can be part of no more than one life connection simultaneously. When the spell is complete, you gain the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.5V49l0K460CcvBOO]{Defend Life} reaction; once you use the reaction, the spell ends.
