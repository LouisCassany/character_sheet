You can add a crystalline additive to an alchemical bomb to have it undermine your enemy. Choose @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.TkIyaNPgTZFBCCuh]{Dazzled}, @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.9PR9y0bi4JPKnHPR]{Deafened}, @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.AJh5ex99aV6VTggg]{Off-Guard}, or a –5-foot status penalty to Speeds. If the attack with the bomb hits, in addition to the bomb's normal effects, the target must succeed at a @Check[fortitude|against:class] saving throw or take that effect until the start of your next turn. Use your class DC for this saving throw, even if someone else throws the bomb.
