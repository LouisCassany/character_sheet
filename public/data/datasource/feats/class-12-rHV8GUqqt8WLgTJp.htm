Name: Thaumaturge's Demesne Nom: Protectorat du thaumaturge État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You have claimed an area or location as your demesne, granting you the ability to ward and protect it. When you select this feat, choose a demesne, an area of no more than 2,000 square feet. You must legitimately own the area or, if its a public area, you must lay claim to the area without anyone contesting it.

Once you've chosen your demesne, it's automatically protected by an arcane @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.onjZCEHs3JJJRTD0]{Peaceful Bubble} spell with an unlimited duration, heightened to half your level rounded up and using your thaumaturge class DC in place of a spell DC, if necessary. Additionally, the demesne is attended by three unseen custodians, as a successful @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.uuoPmbjEtqzWZs0v]{Unseen Custodians} ritual, and one object in the area gains an elemental sentinel, as a successful @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.rOwOLlfVgLMg1FND]{Elemental Sentinel} ritual.
