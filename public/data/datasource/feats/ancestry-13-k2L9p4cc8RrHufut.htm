Name: Look But Don't Touch Nom: Regarde mais ne touche pas État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Frequency once per day

Many flowers are beautiful to look upon but deadly to touch—and you're one of them. For 1 minute, any creature that touches you or damages you with a melee weapon without the reach trait or with a melee unarmed attack takes @Damage[3d6[poison]] damage.
