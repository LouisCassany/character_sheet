Frequency Once per hour

You have uncovered occult mysteries that allow you to unleash the devastating hunger of Kabriri upon your foes with a single exhalation, releasing a massive cloud of flesh-eating gas that glows with a sickly green light. You deal @Damage[7d6[acid],7d6[void]]{7d6 acid and 7d6 void damage} to all creatures within a @Template[cone|distance:30] (@Check[reflex|against:class-spell|basic] save with a DC equal to your class DC or spell DC, whichever is higher). A creature that gets a critical failure on this Reflex save is also @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.6uEgoh53GbXuHpTF]{Paralyzed} for 1 round. A creature that succeeds at this save becomes temporarily immune to Breath of Hungry Death for 24 hours.

When a creature is killed using Breath of Hungry Death, its body is reduced to a smoldering pile of ash wafting with necrotic fumes. You can use a reaction to immediately inhale these fumes and become satiated or fully satiated as if you had used Consume Flesh.

Fréquence Une fois par heure

Vous avez découvert des mystères occultes qui vous permettent de déchaîner la faim dévastatrice de Kabriri sur vos ennemis par une seule expiration, libérant un énorme nuage de gaz mangeur de chair qui brille d'une lumière verte malsaine. Vous pouvez utiliser une réaction pour inhaler immédiatement ces fumées et être rassasié ou complètement rassasié comme si vous aviez utilisé Dévorer la chair.
