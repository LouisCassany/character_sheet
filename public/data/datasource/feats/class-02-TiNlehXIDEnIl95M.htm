Name: Sap Life Nom: Saper la vie État: officielle -- Desc (en) --

You draw the life force out of your enemies. When you cast a @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.wdA52JJnsuQWeyqz]{Harm} spell and damage at least one living creature, you regain Hit Points equal to the spell rank of your harm spell. If you aren't a living creature, you gain no benefit from this feat.
