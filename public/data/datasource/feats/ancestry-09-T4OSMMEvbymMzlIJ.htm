Name: Rejuvenation Token Nom: Jeton de récupération État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You tie yourself to the Material Plane by implanting a piece of your consciousness into a weapon, piece of armor, or other item that's precious to you. As long as this object is intact, even if your body is destroyed, you can be restored to the exact state you were in the last time you made daily preparations with this object in your possession. The soulbound object can be used as a special focus for @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.IkGYwHRLhkuoGReG]{Raise Dead}, @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.kqhPt9344UkcGVYO]{Resurrect}, or similar magic. This warps the spell to bring you back in your skeletal undead state rather than bringing you back to life. Pharasma can't prevent you from returning when the spell is modified in this way.

When you return, you permanently lose a memory that was precious to you, in addition to any other side effects of the spell that brought you back. Your soulbound object becomes inert until you spend 1 week of downtime to anchor yourself to it again, further fragmenting your mind.

L'objet lié à l'âme peut être utilisé comme réceptacle spécial pour @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.IkGYwHRLhkuoGReG]{Rappel à la vie}, @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.kqhPt9344UkcGVYO]{Résurrection} ou toute autre magie similaire. Votre objet lié à l'âme devient inerte jusqu'à ce que vous passiez une semaine d'intermède pour vous y ancrer à nouveau, fragmentant davantage votre esprit.
