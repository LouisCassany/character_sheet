You gain access to @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.equipment-srd.Item.Ak3oHoPDJoMR14yg]{Lion Scythes} and @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.equipment-srd.Item.zWp7Kcn7mhKyGFxW]{Sun Slings}.
