Name: Remediate Nom: Rééquilibrer État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Frequency once per hour

If your next action is to use your divine font to cast a 3-action @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.rfZpqmj0AIIdkVIs]{Heal} or @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.wdA52JJnsuQWeyqz]{Harm} spell, you can harness the residual energy to counterbalance opposing forces. In addition to its normal effects, you use your heal or harm spell to attempt to counteract one divine effect in the spell's area.
