You have a @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.equipment-srd.Item.nza9skYTNtJe2Wd3]{Keepsake} that grants you luck. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to saves against spells and magical effects at all times, not just when you use @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.fYVFBnv9aVHv1UNg]{Leshy Superstition}.
