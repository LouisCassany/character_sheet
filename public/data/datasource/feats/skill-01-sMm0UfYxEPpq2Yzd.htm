Name: Experienced Professional Nom: Professionnel expérimenté État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You carefully safeguard your professional endeavors to prevent disaster. When you use Lore to @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.QyzlsLrqM0EEwd7j]{Earn Income}, if you roll a critical failure, you instead get a failure.
