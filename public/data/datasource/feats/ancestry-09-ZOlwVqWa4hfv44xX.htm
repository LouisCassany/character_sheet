Name: Hero's Wings Nom: Ailes du héros État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Your wings have grown to their full, majestic form, granting you the unlimited power of flight. You have a fly Speed of 25 feet at all times. Furthermore, sprites recognize you as a powerful hero (or villain) by your unique wings and are appropriately impressed. You gain a +2 circumstance bonus on all Diplomacy and Intimidation checks against sprites and gain the benefits of the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.cc8O47KFsODReoBe]{Glad-Hand} skill feat against other sprites, without taking a –5 penalty to your Diplomacy check.
