Name: Quick Fix Nom: Fixer rapidement État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You can attach a talisman using only a bit of glue and some string. You gain the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.x9xA8P2Vlz98He7C]{Rapid Affixture} skill feat, even if you don't meet the prerequisites. When you use it, you can affix or remove up to four talismans in 1 minute instead of just one.

You gain the ability to @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.UAaQk93a30nx0nYY]{Affix a Talisman} as a 3-action activity from that feat at 12th level, regardless of your Crafting proficiency.
