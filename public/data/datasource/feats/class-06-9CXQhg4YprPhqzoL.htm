You tumble around your foes, expertly avoiding their reactions. Stride up to half your Speed and roll an @Check[acrobatics] check. Compare the result to the Reflex DC of each enemy whose reach you began in or enter during the movement, in sequence.

Critical Success This movement doesn't trigger reactions from the enemy, and the enemy is @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.AJh5ex99aV6VTggg]{Off-Guard} to you until the end of your turn
