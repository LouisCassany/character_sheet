Name: See You in Hell Nom: On se revoit en Enfer État: changé Changés: desc État d'origine: libre -- Desc (en) --

Frequency once per day

Trigger You are reduced to 0 Hit points by a creature but not immediately killed.

You aren't sure if you'll be reincarnated again, so you might as well take this foe with you. A blast of spiritual energy lashes out from the depths of your soul, targeting the creature who attacked you. The creature takes mental damage equal to the amount that reduced you to 0 Hit Points (basic @Check[will|against:class-spell] save with a DC equal to your class DC or spell DC, whichever is higher).

Fréquence Une fois par jour
