Name: Reflect Foe Nom: Ennemi reflété État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You create a copy of a foe by calling them forth from a nearby reflection, clonally growing them in an instant from one of your hairs or otherwise creating them in a way that matches your own nature as a reflection. You can cast @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.73rToy0v5Ra9NvL6]{Duplicate Foe} once per day as an innate occult spell. At 20th level, your innate duplicate foe is heightened to 8th level.
