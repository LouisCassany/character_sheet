Name: Lunging Stance Nom: Posture de fente État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Requirements You are wielding a melee weapon.

Your body coiled to strike, you can lash out at distant enemies. While you are in this stance, you can use @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.KAVf7AmRnbCAHrkT]{Reactive Strike} against a creature that is outside your reach but within the reach you would have with a @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.rTkr1EqpAN6YtnAh]{Lunge}. If you do, you increase your range with the Strike by 5 feet.
