Name: Thoughtform Summoning Nom: Invocation mentalisée État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You can create a prototypical creature from the depths of your imagination instead of calling one from elsewhere. Whenever you summon a creature, you can choose to instead create a psychic construct of that creature. This grants it resistance to physical damage equal to half its level, weakness 5 to force and mental damage, and the ability to pass through enemies' spaces without needing to @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.21WIfSu7Xd7uKqV8]{Tumble Through} (though it can't end its turn in an occupied space).

Vous pouvez tirer des profondeurs de votre imagination une créature emblématique au lieu d'en invoquer une provenant d'ailleurs.
