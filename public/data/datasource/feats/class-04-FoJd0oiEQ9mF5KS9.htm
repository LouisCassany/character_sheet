Name: Steady Spellcasting (Magus) Nom: Incantation fiable (magus) État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Confident in your technique, you don't easily lose your concentration when you Cast a Spell.

If a reaction would disrupt your spellcasting action, attempt a @Check[flat|dc:15] check. If you succeed, your action isn't disrupted.
