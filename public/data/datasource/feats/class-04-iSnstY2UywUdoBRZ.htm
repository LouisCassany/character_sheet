A slew of logs eject from the Plane of Wood and slam into your enemies. They roll in a @Template[line|distance:30|width:10]{10-foot-wide, 30-foot-long line}. Non-magical difficult terrain on any ground the logs roll over is smashed flat; greater difficult terrain remains. Each creature in the area takes @Damage[(floor((@actor.level -4)/3)+2)d8[bludgeoning]|traits:area-damage] damage and might be moved by the logs, depending on its @Check[reflex|against:kineticist] save against your class DC.

Critical Success The creature is unaffected.

Success The creature takes half damage.

Failure The creature takes full damage and is pushed out of the line in the most direct path available.

Critical Failure The creature takes double damage and is knocked @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.j91X7x0XSomq8d60]{Prone}.

Level (+3) The damage increases by 1d8.
