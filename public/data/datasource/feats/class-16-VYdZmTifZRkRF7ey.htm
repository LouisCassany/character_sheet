Name: Deflecting Cloud Nom: Nuage déflecteur État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Frequency once every 10 minutes

Trigger You are the target of a ranged attack.

Requirements You are aware of the attack and currently have dragon wings active.

You've mastered a defensive technique unique to the ancient dragons of Bloodsalt: beating your wings to create a billowing cloud of mist. You become momentarily @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.iU0fEDdBp3rXpTMC]{Hidden} from the triggering attack if vision is the attacker's only precise sense. Normally this means the attacker must succeed at a @Check[flat|dc:11|name:Hidden Flat Check|traits:hidden|showDC:owner] check to target you. You also gain a +4 circumstance bonus to AC against the triggering attack.
