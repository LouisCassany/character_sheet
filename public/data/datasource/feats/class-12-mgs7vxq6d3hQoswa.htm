Name: Improved Dueling Riposte Nom: Riposte en duel améliorée État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Your weapon whirls and darts, striking foes whenever the slightest opening or moment of weakness presents itself. You can use @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.mf2cdCRV8uowOMOm]{Dueling Riposte} even if you aren't benefiting from @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.n7nQQR940OvFbw7T]{Dueling Parry}. At the start of each of your turns, you gain an additional reaction that you can use only to make a Dueling Riposte.
