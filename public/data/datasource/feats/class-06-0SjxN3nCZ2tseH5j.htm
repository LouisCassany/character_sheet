Name: Abscission Shards Nom: Éclats d'abscission État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Requirements Your last action was a successful frenzied claw or spinning talon Strike.

You twist your claw, breaking off a piece in your target. The shard deals @Damage[1d6[persistent,bleed]] damage. This increases to 2d6 if your unarmed attacks deal three weapon damage dice, such as under a greater striking rune, and 3d6 if they deal four weapon damage dice.
