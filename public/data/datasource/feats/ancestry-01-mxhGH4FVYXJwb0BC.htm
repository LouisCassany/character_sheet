Name: Gnome Obsession Nom: Obsession Gnome État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You might have a flighty nature, but when a topic captures your attention, you dive into it headfirst. You gain the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.BocFD2KV0qgUC76x]{Additional Lore} feat and the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.W6Gl9ePmItfDHji0]{Assurance} feat for the chosen Lore. As gnome obsessions can come and go quickly, you can retrain Gnome Obsession to a different Lore subcategory in 1 day of downtime.
