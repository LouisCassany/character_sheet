You can enter a second rage, but afterward you need to catch your breath. When you Rage, you can choose to gain temporary HP from the Rage even if 1 minute hasn't passed (or a full turn if you have the revitalizing rage class feature). If you do, once this Rage ends you become @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.HL2l2VRSaQHu9lUw]{Fatigued} until you rest for 10 minutes.
