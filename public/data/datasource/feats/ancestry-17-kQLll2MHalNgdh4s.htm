You've mastered your defensive pheromones and have nearly complete control over them. When you Emit Defensive Odor, you can either choose to increase the damage dealt to a single target by changing the damage dice to d8s (@Damage[(floor((@actor.level +1)/2)+1)d8[poison]]), or leave the damage unchanged but spray your pheromones in a @Template[cone|distance:15].
