Name: Born to the Trees Nom: Né pour les arbres État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You adapt a creature to live among the trees, improving its ability to climb, balance, and leap. Target a willing creature within 30 feet. For 10 minutes, it gains a climb Speed equal to its Speed and a +1 circumstance bonus to Acrobatics checks to @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.M76ycLAqHoAgbcej]{Balance} and Athletics checks to @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.JUvAvruz7yRQXfz2]{Long Jump} and @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.2HJ4yuEFY1Cast4h]{High Jump}. When it Leaps, it increases the distance it can @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.d5I6018Mci2SWokk]{Leap} horizontally by 5 feet and vertically by 2 feet. If you use Born to the Trees again, any existing one ends.

@UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feat-effects.Item.uaWIJU1m2eHgfkDp]{Effect: Born to the Trees}

Level (6th) You can target up to 5 willing creatures.
