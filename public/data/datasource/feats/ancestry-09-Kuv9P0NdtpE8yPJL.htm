Name: Solar Rejuvenation (Ghoran) Nom: Récupération solaire (Ghoran) État: libre -- Desc (en) --

The warmth and light of the sun gives you life. If you rest outdoors for 10 minutes during the day, you regain Hit Points equal to your [[/r (floor(@actor.level/2)*@actor.con.mod)[healing]]]{Constitution modifier × half your level}. You gain this benefit in addition to any healing from @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.1kGNdIIhuglAjIp9]{Treat Wounds}.
