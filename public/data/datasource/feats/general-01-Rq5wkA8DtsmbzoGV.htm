Name: Ride Nom: Chevaucher État: libre -- Desc (en) --

When you @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.q9nbyIF0PEBqMtYe]{Command an Animal} you're mounted on to take a move action (such as Stride), you automatically succeed instead of needing to attempt a check. Any animal you're mounted on acts on your turn, like a minion. If you @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.PM5jvValFkbFH3TV]{Mount} an animal in the middle of an encounter, it skips its next turn and then acts on your next turn.
