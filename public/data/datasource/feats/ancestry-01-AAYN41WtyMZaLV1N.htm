Name: Native Waters Nom: Eaux natives État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You were born with or obtained a special connection to either fresh water or salt water. Once made, this decision can't be changed. Whenever you take your full rest in a natural body of water of the type corresponding to your connection, you recover @Damage[(@actor.level)[healing]]{additional Hit Points} equal to your level, and if you have the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.3uh1r86TzbQvosxv]{Doomed} or @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.HL2l2VRSaQHu9lUw]{Fatigued} conditions, you reduce them by 2 instead of 1.
