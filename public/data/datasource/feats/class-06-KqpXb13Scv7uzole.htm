Name: Tree of Duality Nom: Arbre de dualité État: changé Changés: desc État d'origine: libre -- Desc (en) --

An ephemeral tree bursts forth, floating on air in an unoccupied square of your choice within 60 feet. Blooming flowers and fungal growths shed pollen and spores, which a swirling gust of air spreads in a @Template[emanation|distance:10] around it. The tree lasts until the end of your next turn, and you can Sustain it up to 1 minute. This has both of the following effects.

  • Cleansing Pollen (healing, vitality) Each living ally that's in the area or enters it regains 3d4 HP and is then temporarily immune to regaining HP from Tree of Duality for 10 minutes.
  • Hallucinogenic Spores (mental) Enemies in the area are @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.TkIyaNPgTZFBCCuh]{Dazzled}. An enemy that leaves the area remains dazzled until the start of its next turn.

Level (+2) The healing increases by 1d4.
