You can use the oaths bound in your ka stone to compel the truth from the living and the dead. You can activate your ka stone to target a single living creature, causing that creature to experience the effects of being in a @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.aewxsale5xWEPKLk]{Ring of Truth}. You can also activate it with a 10-minute activation (envision) to cast @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.FM3SmEW8N1FCRjqt]{Talking Corpse}.
