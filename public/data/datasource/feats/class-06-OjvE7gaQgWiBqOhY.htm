Name: Discerning Strike Nom: Frappe sagace État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Requirements You have successfully identified the target with @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.1OagaWtBpVXExToo]{Recall Knowledge} during this combat.

Identifying a creature is the first step in finding its weaknesses. Attempt a Strike against the target; if successful, you deal an additional 1d6 precision damage. If you have succeeded on a Recall Knowledge check against the target during this combat and their creature type was previously recorded using @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.0Dy8RlFqrzCVOTl4]{Thorough Reports}, you also deal 1 point of persistent bleed damage. The creature is then temporarily immune to Discerning Strike for 1 day.
