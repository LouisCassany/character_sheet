Name: Night's Glow Nom: Lueur de la nuit État: libre -- Desc (en) --

The stars and moon lend you their light. You gain your choice of the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.SuBtUJiU6DbSJYIw]{Moonbeam} or @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.Yrek2Yd4k3DPC2zV]{Zenith Star} domain spell. Like your Oatia skysage spells, you cast this focus spell as an occult spell. It costs 1 Focus Point to cast a focus spell. This feat grants a focus pool of 1 Focus Point that you can recover by using the Refocus to reflect upon or gaze at the stars.

Special You can take this feat a second time, gaining the focus spell that you didn't gain the first time. Increase the number of Focus Points in your pool by 1.

Les étoiles et la lune vous prêtent leur lumière.
