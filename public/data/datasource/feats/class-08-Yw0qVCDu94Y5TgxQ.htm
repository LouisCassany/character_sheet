Name: Predictive Purchase (Rogue) Nom: Achat intuitif (Roublard) État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You somehow manage to have just the thing for the situation. You gain the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.bh2jHyyYrkLMsIdX]{Prescient Planner} and @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.lHFz4MmebvPqTb0A]{Prescient Consumable} feats. When using Prescient Planner, you can draw the item from your backpack (or a similar container) as a 2-action activity, instead of taking 1 minute to do so.
