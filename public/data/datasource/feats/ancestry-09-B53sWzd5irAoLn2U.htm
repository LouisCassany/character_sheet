Name: Bone Caller Nom: Invocateur d'os État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Using a mixture of dinosaur bones and the bones of your ancestors to focus your magic, you animate lizards and dinosaurs to carry messages or serve as mounts. You can cast @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.yhz9fF69uwRhnHix]{Animal Messenger} and @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.WPKJOhEihhcIm2uQ]{Marvelous Mount} as primal innate spells once per day each. The animal messenger is always a Tiny dinosaur or lizard, and your innate spell transforms it from bones to flesh, even if there are no dinosaurs or lizards nearby. The phantom steed looks like a dinosaur rather than a horse but functions identically.
