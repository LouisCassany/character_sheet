Name: Gunslinger Dedication Nom: Dévouement : Franc-tireur État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You become trained in simple and martial crossbows and simple and martial firearms. You become trained in gunslinger class DC. Choose a @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.classfeatures.Item.LDqVxLKrwEqSegiu]{Gunslinger Way}. You become trained in your way's associated skill; if you were already trained in this skill, you become trained in a skill of your choice. You don't gain any other abilities from your choice of way.

Special You can't select another dedication feat until you've gained two other feats from the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.journals.JournalEntry.vx5FGEG34AxI2dow.JournalEntryPage.CSVoyUvynmM5LzPW]{Gunslinger} archetype.
