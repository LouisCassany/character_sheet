Trigger You are @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.iU0fEDdBp3rXpTMC]{Hidden} and a creature who hasn't detected you passes within 20 feet of you.

Like the moray eels athamaru communities raise and train, you know how to quickly strike from hiding. Stride or Swim directly toward the triggering creature and make a melee Strike against it.
