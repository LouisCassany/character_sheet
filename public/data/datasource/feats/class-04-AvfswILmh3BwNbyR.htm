Name: Basic Sorcerer Spellcasting Nom: Incantation basique de l'ensorceleur État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You gain the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.journals.JournalEntry.vx5FGEG34AxI2dow.JournalEntryPage.DOc3Pf8wmVxanTIv#basic-spellcasting-feat]{Basic Spellcasting Feat} benefits. Each time you gain a spell slot of a new level from the sorcerer archetype, add a spell of the appropriate spell rank to your repertoire: a common spell of your bloodline's tradition, one of your bloodline's granted spells, or another spell you have learned or discovered.
