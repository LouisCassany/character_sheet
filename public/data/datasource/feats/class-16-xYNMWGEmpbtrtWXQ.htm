Name: Unwind Death Nom: Remonter la mort État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Requirements The target's body must be mostly intact.

Combining necromancy with the mysterious temporal powers of clockwork, you wind a creature backward from the moment of its death. Attempt a @Check[crafting|dc:40] check to revive a dead creature that has been dead for no more than 3 rounds. If you succeed, the target returns to life with the effects of @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.IkGYwHRLhkuoGReG]{Raise Dead}, except it still has the wounded condition it had before dying, increased by 1 (or wounded 1 if it wasn't wounded before dying). Whether you succeed or fail, the target is then temporarily immune to Unwind Death for 1 day.
