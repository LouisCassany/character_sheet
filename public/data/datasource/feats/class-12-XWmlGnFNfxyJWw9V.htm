Name: Choking Smoke Nom: Fumée étouffante État: libre -- Desc (en) --

When you cast @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.sxQZ6yqTn0czJxVd]{Fireball} heightened to at least 6th level, you can modify the spell's standard effects as follows: Reduce the spell's damage by 6d6. Your fireball leaves behind a lingering cloud of toxic smoke in its area. While it's smoke instead of mist, this cloud otherwise has the effects of @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.GKpEcy9WId6NJvtx]{Stinking Cloud}.
