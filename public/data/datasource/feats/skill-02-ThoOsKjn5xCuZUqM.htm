Name: Quick Contacts Nom: Contacts rapides État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You know where to go, who to talk to, and how to make new connections, fast. Upon entering a new settlement, spending 1 day of downtime allows you to build enough connections to make use of the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.iCv8KTZ5PcF4GqeV]{Connections} or @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.LDUWw2TeEEH0KA6M]{Criminal Connections} feats. If you're legendary in Society, you can form the required connections within 1 hour of entering a new settlement.
