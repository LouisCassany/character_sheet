Name: Liberating Stride Nom: Foulée libératrice État: officielle -- Desc (en) --

Instead of you taking a Step at the end of your @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.IX1VlVCL5sFTptEE]{Liberating Step}, the triggering ally can Stride up to half their Speed.

Even if you have @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.classfeatures.Item.uptzvOLrZ3fctrl2]{Exalt}, only the triggering ally gains this benefit.
