The physical growth of your transformation is a spectacle that shakes foes to their core. Each enemy smaller than your new size within 30 feet of you must attempt a @Check[will|against:class-spell] save against your class DC or spell DC, whichever is higher.

Success The creature is unaffected.

Failure The creature becomes @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.TBSHQspnbcqxsmjL]{Frightened 1}.
