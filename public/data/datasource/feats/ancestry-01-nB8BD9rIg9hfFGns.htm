Name: Eye For Treasure Nom: Oeil appréciateur État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You know good artisanship when you see it and can wax poetic about crafting techniques and forms.

You become trained in Crafting and gain a +1 circumstance bonus on all Crafting checks made to @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.1OagaWtBpVXExToo]{Recall Knowledge}. If you would automatically become trained in Crafting (from your background or class, for example), you instead become trained in a skill of your choice.

In addition, you gain the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.voYr7ygVcWmlg1f4]{Crafter's Appraisal} skill feat, enabling you to identify magic items using the Crafting skill.
