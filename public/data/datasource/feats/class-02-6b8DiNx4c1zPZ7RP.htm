Name: Edgewatch Detective Dedication Nom: Dévouement : Officier de la Garde du Précipice État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You become trained in Society or Thievery; if you are already trained in both of these skills, you instead become trained in a skill of your choice. You can use Perception instead of Survival to [[/act track statistic=perception]]{Track}, and you gain the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.urQZwmzg2kS53vd5]{Experienced Tracker} skill feat.

Special You can't select another dedication feat until you have gained two other feats from the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.journals.JournalEntry.vx5FGEG34AxI2dow.JournalEntryPage.5RH1CkZQHYpsOium]{Edgewatch Detective} archetype.
