You slam your hands together to unleash a deafening blast. Creatures in a @Template[cone|distance:15] cone take @Damage[(max(3,floor(@actor.level/2)))d6[sonic]] damage, with a @Check[fortitude|against:class|basic] against your class DC. Creatures that critically fail their save are also @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.9PR9y0bi4JPKnHPR]{Deafened} for 1 minute.
