Name: Sickening Bite Nom: Morsure répugnante État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Your saliva causes severe nausea and mild fever in the living, weakening them so that you can more easily finish them off. When you critically hit a creature with your jaws, the creature is @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.fesd1n5eVhpCSS18]{Sickened 1}. This is a disease effect.

If you are satiated, you can end your satiation to make the target @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.fesd1n5eVhpCSS18]{Sickened 1} on a regular hit instead.
