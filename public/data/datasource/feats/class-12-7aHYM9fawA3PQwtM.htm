Trigger Your reanimated clockwork companion is reduced to 0 Hit Points.

Requirements You're within 60 feet of your reanimated clockwork companion.

You've installed a self-destruct device within your construct companion, which you can trigger with a special device from up to 60 feet away. When you activate the device, your reanimated construct companion explodes, dealing @Damage[2d6[fire]] damage per level to all creatures in a @Template[emanation|distance:30]{30-foot radius}. Creatures caught in the blast can attempt a @Check[reflex|against:class-spell] save, with a DC equal to the higher of your class DC or spell DC.
