You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to saving throws against effects with the darkness, light, or shadow traits, and whenever you become @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.TkIyaNPgTZFBCCuh]{Dazzled} or @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.XgEqL1kFApUbl5Z2]{Blinded} for at least 2 rounds, you can reduce the duration by 1 round.
