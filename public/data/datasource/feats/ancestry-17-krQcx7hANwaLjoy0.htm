Name: Into the Labyrinth Nom: Dans le labyrinthe État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Calling on your connection to minotaurs of myth, you shunt an enemy into a complex maze full of puzzles of your own devising. Once per day, you can cast @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.Oj1PJBMQD9vuwCv7]{Quandary} as an 8th-rank occult innate spell, which takes the form of a labyrinth instead of a puzzle room. Targets cannot attempt to use Occultism to escape the spell (as normal for quandary), but they can use Survival to find their way through the labyrinth's corridors.
