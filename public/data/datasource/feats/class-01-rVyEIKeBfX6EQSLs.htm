Name: Tidal Hands Nom: Mains des marées État: changé Changés: desc État d'origine: libre -- Desc (en) --

With an emphatic gesture, you create waves that rush out from you in the shape of your hands. You either form two @Template[cone|distance:15]s that don't overlap or you combine the hands into one @Template[cone|distance:30]. Each creature in a wave takes @Damage[(floor((@actor.level -1)/2)+1)d8[bludgeoning]|traits:area-damage] damage with a @Check[reflex|against:kineticist|basic] save against your class DC. If a creature critically fails, you also push that creature 5 feet.

Level (+2) The damage increases by 1d8.
