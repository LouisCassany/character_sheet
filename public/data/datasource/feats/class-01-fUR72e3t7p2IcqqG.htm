Name: Divine Disharmony Nom: Désaccord divin État: libre -- Desc (en) --

From your collection of religious trinkets, you pull out opposing divine objects-such as the religious symbols of two deities that are hated enemies-and combine them in a display that causes discordant clashes of divine energy that are especially distracting to the faithful. Roll your choice of a @Check[deception|defense:will] or @Check[intimidation|defense:will] check against the Will DC of a creature you can see within 60 feet, with the following results. If the creature is particularly devoted to a deity, such as a cleric, celestial, monitor, fiend, or other creature with divine spells, you gain a +2 circumstance bonus to your skill check. The GM might determine that a creature that despises all deities, such as an alghollthu, is unaffected.

Critical Success The creature is @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.AJh5ex99aV6VTggg]{Off-Guard} to your attacks until the end of your next turn.

Success The creature is off-guard against your attacks until the end of your current turn.
