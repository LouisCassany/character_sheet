Name: Reinforced Chassis Nom: Châssis renforcé État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Your body is designed to be particularly resilient. Your chassis is medium armor in the plate armor group that grants a +4 item bonus to AC, a Dex cap of +1, a check penalty of -2, a speed penalty of -5 feet, and a Strength value of +3. You can never wear other armor or remove your chassis; however, you still don't become @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.HL2l2VRSaQHu9lUw]{Fatigued} from sleeping. Finally, you can etch armor runes onto your chassis as normal.

Enhancement Your chassis becomes difficult to overcome. You gain the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.uOlyklPCUWLtCaYI]{Chassis Deflection} reaction.
