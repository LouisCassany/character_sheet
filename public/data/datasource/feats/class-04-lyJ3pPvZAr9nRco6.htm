Name: Hallowed Initiate Nom: Initié sacré État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You've expanded your focus spellcasting to techniques used by clerics and wizards. You gain an additional focus spell, either the necromancer school spell @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.BH3sUerzMb2bWnv1]{Call of the Grave} or the initial domain spell of the death domain, @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.HG4afO9EOGEU9bZN]{Death's Call}. Increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1.

Special You can select this feat a second time, choosing the other initial focus spell.
