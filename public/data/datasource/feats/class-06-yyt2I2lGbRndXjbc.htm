Name: Selective Energy Nom: Énergie sélective État: libre -- Desc (en) --

As you call down divine power, you can prevent some enemies from benefiting or some allies from being hurt. When you cast a version of @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.wdA52JJnsuQWeyqz]{Harm} or @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.rfZpqmj0AIIdkVIs]{Heal} that has an area, you can designate up to 5 creatures in the area. They are not targeted by the spell.
