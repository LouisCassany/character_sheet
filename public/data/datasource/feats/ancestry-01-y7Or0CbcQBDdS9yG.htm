Name: Elven Aloofness Nom: Détachement elfique État: libre -- Desc (en) --

As much as you might care for them, you've come to terms with the ephemeral nature of non-elves, and it makes their threats feel less troublesome.

If a non-elf rolls a failure on a check to @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.tHCqgwjtQtzNqVvd]{Coerce} you using Intimidation, it gets a critical failure instead (and thus can't try to Coerce you again for 1 week).

When a non-elf attempts to @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.2u915NdUyQan6uKF]{Demoralize} you, you become temporarily immune for 1 day, instead of 10 minutes.
