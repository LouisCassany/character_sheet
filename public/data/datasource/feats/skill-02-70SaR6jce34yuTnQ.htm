Name: What's That Up Your Sleeve Nom: Qu'est-ce qu'il y a dans ta manche ? État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Whether as a dealer or player, you've learned to spot cheaters. When another creature attempts to @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.qVNVSmsgpKFGk9hV]{Conceal an Object} from you, use your Gambling Lore DC if it's higher than your Perception DC to determine whether they succeed. When you search a creature for a @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.DmAIPqOBomZ7H95W]{Concealed} object, you can use Gambling Lore in place of Perception.
