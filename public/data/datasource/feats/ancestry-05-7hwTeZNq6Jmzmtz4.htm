When you terrorize your enemies, you also cause them painful mental distress. When you successfully @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.2u915NdUyQan6uKF]{Demoralize} an enemy, that enemy takes @Damage[ternary(gte(@actor.system.skills.intimidation.rank,4),4,ternary(gte(@actor.system.skills.intimidation.rank,3),2,1))d4[mental]] damage at the start of each of its turns.
