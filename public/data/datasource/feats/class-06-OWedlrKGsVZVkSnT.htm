Your understanding of life expands, allowing you to mimic a wider range of creatures. Add the shapes in @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.XI6Lzd2B5pernkPd]{Insect Form} to your @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.8RWfKConLYFZpQ9X]{Untamed Form} list.
