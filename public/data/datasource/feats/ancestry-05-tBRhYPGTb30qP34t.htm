Requirements You are standing on the ground, and there are plants rooted in the ground within 100 feet of you.

The root systems of trees are a far-reaching network of information, and you know how to access them and extract their secrets. You @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.BlAOM2X92SI6HMtJ]{Seek} within 30 feet using tremorsense, an imprecise sense, instead of one of your own senses.
