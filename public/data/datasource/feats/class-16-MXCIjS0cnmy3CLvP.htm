Name: Too Much to Swallow Nom: Trop à avaler État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Trigger You're subject to an effect you could attempt to @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.SkZAQRkLLkmBQNB9]{Escape}.

While you might be small enough to grab normally, you can change that at a moment's notice. You cast @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.8RWfKConLYFZpQ9X]{Untamed Form}. If you took on a new form that's too large for the effect, you automatically escape. Otherwise, you attempt to Escape. If this ends an effect where you entered the creature's space, such as @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.bestiary-ability-glossary-srd.Item.uJSseLa57HZYSMUu]{Swallow Whole}, exit that creature to an adjacent area where your new form could fit.
