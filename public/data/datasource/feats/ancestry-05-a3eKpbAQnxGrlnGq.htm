Name: Restoring Blood Nom: Sang récupérateur État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Your body can process an alternative to your venom that heals wounds. You can use your @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.s4V7JWSMF9JPJAeX]{Envenom} action to produce a blood restorative instead of vishkanyan venom. You can make a blood restorative at most once per 10 minutes, even if your Envenom action can be used more frequently. You or an adjacent creature can Interact to consume the restorative to regain @Damage[(ternary(gte(@actor.level,15),5,3))d6[vitality,healing]|shortLabel] Hit Points. This is a vitality healing effect. A restorative spoils at the start of your next turn. When you reach 15th level, the restorative restores 5d6 Hit Points instead.

Lorsque vous atteignez le niveau 15, le reconstituant permet de récupérer 5d6 points de vie à la place.
