Name: Inexhaustible Countermoves Nom: Contremesures infatigables État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You can react whenever a foe leaves a gap in their defenses. At the start of each enemy's turn, you gain an extra reaction you can use only during that turn to perform an @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.classfeatures.Item.Jtn7IugykXDlIoZq]{Opportune Riposte}, or an @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.classfeatures.Item.hmShTfPOcTaKgbf4]{Reactive Strike} if you have that reaction.
