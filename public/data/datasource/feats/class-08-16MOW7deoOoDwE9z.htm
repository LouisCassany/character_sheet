Name: Hazard Finder Nom: Dénicheur de dangers État: officielle -- Desc (en) --

You have an intuitive ability to sense hazards. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to Perception checks to find traps and hazards, to AC against their attacks, and to saves against their effects. You can find hazards that would normally require you to @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.TiNDYUGlMmxzxBYU]{Search} even if you aren't Searching.
