You are luckier even than most catfolk. You can trigger @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.7qBuzHY8kEG8SdEP]{Cat's Luck} when you fail or critically fail a Fortitude saving throw, Will saving throw, Acrobatics check, or Athletics check in addition to Reflex saving throws.
