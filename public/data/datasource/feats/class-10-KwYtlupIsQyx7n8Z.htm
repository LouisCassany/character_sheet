Name: Elemental Transformation Nom: Transformation élémentaire État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Frequency once per day

Requirements Your kinetic gate is deactivated.

You open your kinetic gate, but rather than activating your kinetic aura, you instead become overwhelmed with transformative elemental energy. You're affected by an @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.1K6AYGisvo9gqdhs]{Elemental Form} spell with the same trait as your kinetic element. You can heighten it to any spell rank up to half your level rounded up.
