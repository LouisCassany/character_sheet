Name: Lingering Chill Nom: Froid persistant État: libre -- Desc (en) --

The chill of ice entered your veins and never left, adjusting the internal temperature of your body. Your breath comes out in puffs, and your skin is as cold and smooth as the ice in your veins. You can cast @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.gYjPm7YwGtEa1oxh]{Ray of Frost} as a primal innate cantrip.
