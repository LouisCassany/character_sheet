Name: Eidetic Ear Nom: Oreille éidétique État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You have an amazing memory for sound. You gain the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.W6Gl9ePmItfDHji0]{Assurance (Performance)} feat. With a successful @Check[flat|dc:8], you can accurately recall a sound or a snippet of conversation you heard within the last week.
