Name: Instinctive Obfuscation Nom: Obscurcissement instinctif État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Frequency once per hour

Trigger You are attacked by a foe, but they haven't rolled yet.

The magic within you manifests as a natural reaction to threats. An illusory double of you appears in your space for a brief moment. The triggering attacker must roll a @Check[flat|dc:10]; on a success, the attack targets you normally; if they fail, the attack targets the double and destroys it. The tradition of this action matches the tradition of your gnome ancestry options.
