Name: Blessed Blood (Nephilim) Nom: Sang béni (Néphilim) État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Your spilled blood is sanctified, with effects similar to those of @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.equipment-srd.Item.z9T4c1hXwOotsMCp]{Holy Water}. Whenever a fiend, undead, or creature with a weakness to holy drinks your blood or deals piercing or slashing damage to you with jaws, fangs, or a similar attack, that creature takes @Damage[1d6[spirit]|traits:holy] damage with the holy trait. You gain a +4 circumstance bonus to Crafting checks to @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.rmwa3OyhTZ2i2AHl]{Craft} holy water using your blood as one of the ingredients.
