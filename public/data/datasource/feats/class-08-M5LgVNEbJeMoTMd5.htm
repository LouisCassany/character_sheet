Name: Ears of the Forest Nom: Oreilles de la forêt État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Beyond relying on your own senses, you keep an ear open for nearby creatures for help in exploring your surroundings. When you @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.BlAOM2X92SI6HMtJ]{Seek}, if there are any willing animals in the selected area, you can borrow their senses. Doing so lets you Seek with scent and tremorsense as imprecise senses, along with any other senses appropriate to the animals in the area, at the GM's discretion.
