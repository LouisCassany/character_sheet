Name: Gorilla Pound Nom: Martèlement du gorille État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Requirements You are in @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.DqD7htz8Sd1dh3BT]{Gorilla Stance}.

You pound your chest before slamming into your foes.

Attempt an Intimidation check to @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.2u915NdUyQan6uKF]{Demoralize}, then make one gorilla slam Strike against the same target. If your Strike hits, you gain a circumstance bonus to the damage roll equal to triple the value of the target's @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.TBSHQspnbcqxsmjL]{Frightened} condition.

Special If you have this feat, while you are in Gorilla Stance, you gain a @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.pprgrYQ1QnIDGZiy]{Climb} Speed of 15 feet.
