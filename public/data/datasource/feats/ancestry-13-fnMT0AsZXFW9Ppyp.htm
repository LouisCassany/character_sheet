Name: Cloak Of Poison Nom: Cape de poison État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Frequency once per day

You secrete a cloak of concentrated poison that harms any who dare attack you. For 1 minute, any creature that touches you or damages you with an unarmed attack or melee weapon without the reach trait takes @Damage[3d6[poison]] damage.
