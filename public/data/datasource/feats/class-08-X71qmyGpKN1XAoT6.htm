Name: Shamble Nom: Tituber État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You may be slower than you were in life, but your pace is unfaltering. Stride twice, or three times if you spent 3 actions to Shamble. During this movement, you ignore difficult terrain and greater difficult terrain.

You can choose to ignore all circumstance and status penalties to your Speed when you Shamble. If you do, you must succeed at a @Check[flat|dc:6] when you finish Shambling or your body deteriorates.
