Name: Warden's Step Nom: Foulée du protecteur État: officielle -- Desc (en) --

You can guide your allies to move quietly through the wilderness. When you @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.IE2nThCmoyhQA0Jn]{Avoid Notice} during exploration in natural terrain, you can designate any number of your allies to gain the benefits as if they were using that activity during that exploration.
