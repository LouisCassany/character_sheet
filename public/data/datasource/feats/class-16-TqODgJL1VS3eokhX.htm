Name: Swift Elusion Nom: Échappatoire rapide État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Trigger A foe ends its movement adjacent to you.

Attempt an @Check[acrobatics|defense:reflex] check against the foe's Reflex DC. If you succeed, either Stride to a different space adjacent to the foe or move the foe to a different space adjacent to you.
