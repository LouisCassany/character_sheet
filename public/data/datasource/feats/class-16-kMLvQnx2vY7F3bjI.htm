Your deeds have brought your deity's grace to you for all of eternity. You're continuously surrounded by a @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.XSujb7EsSwKl19Uu]{Bless} spell, with a spell rank equal to half your level (rounded up).
