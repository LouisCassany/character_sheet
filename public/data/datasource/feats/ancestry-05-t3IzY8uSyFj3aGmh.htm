Name: Shed Tail Nom: Autotomie caudale État: changé Changés: desc État d'origine: libre -- Desc (en) --

Trigger You become @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.kWc1fhmv9LBiTuei]{Grabbed}.

Requirements You have a fully grown tail.

You can shed a portion of your tail to escape. You cease being grabbed, then Stride without triggering any reactions from the creature that grabbed you. It takes 1 day for your tail to fully grow back. Until it does, you can't use your tail unarmed attack, and you take a –2 circumstance penalty on checks to @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.M76ycLAqHoAgbcej]{Balance}.
