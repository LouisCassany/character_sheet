You've learned how to synchronize traditional martial arts and guns. You gain the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.SMF1hTWPHtmlS8Cd]{Bullet Dancer Stance} action and are trained in bayonets and reinforced stocks.
