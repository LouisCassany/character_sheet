Name: Twin Feint Nom: Feinte jumelée État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Requirements You are wielding two melee weapons, each in a different hand.

You use an attack with one weapon to distract your foe from a second attack coming at a different angle. Make one Strike with each of your two melee weapons, both against the same target. The target is automatically @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.AJh5ex99aV6VTggg]{Off-Guard} against the second attack. Apply your multiple attack penalty to the Strikes normally.
