Name: Current Spell (Elementalist) Nom: Courant de sort (élémentaliste) État: libre -- Desc (en) --

As you use your magic to manipulate air or water, you divert some of its currents to form a barrier around you. If your next action is to @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.aBQ8ajvEBByv45yz]{Cast a Spell} with the air or water trait, until the start of your next turn, you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to AC or a +2 circumstance bonus against ranged attacks. This effect has the air or water trait, or both, depending on the traits of the spell you cast. You also gain a +1 circumstance bonus to all saves against effects with the air trait, water trait, or both until the start of your next turn, depending on the spell's traits.
