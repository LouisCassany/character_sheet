Name: Buzzing Death Cicadas Nom: Bourdonnement des cigales de la mort État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Your swarm takes on characteristics of the death cicadas of the Mana Wastes, whose distinctive buzz is said to be a herald of imminent demise. The inclusion of these death cicadas grants your swarm a fly Speed of 20 feet. You also gain the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.6iGoVnEJuBHJqgpi]{Death Drone} action.
