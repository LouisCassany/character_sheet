Name: Fearsome Brute Nom: Brute effrayante État: officielle -- Desc (en) --

Fear makes your foes weak and more vulnerable to your attacks. You gain a circumstance bonus to damage rolls for Strikes against @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.TBSHQspnbcqxsmjL]{Frightened} creatures. The bonus is equal to double the target's frightened value.

If you're a master in Intimidation, increase the bonus to triple the target's frightened value.
