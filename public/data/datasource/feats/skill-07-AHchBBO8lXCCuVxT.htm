Name: Grudging Compliment Nom: Compliments à contrecoeur État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Trigger An enemy within 30 feet damages you or succeeds at a skill check.

Every Firebrand can recognize the skill of others, but you've turned it into an art you can wield in combat. You compliment a foe's form or technique, and your unexpected praise distracts them from their surroundings. Attempt a @Check[diplomacy|defense:will] check against that enemy's Will DC. Regardless of your result, they are temporarily immune to your Grudging Compliments for 10 minutes.

Critical Success The target becomes @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.AJh5ex99aV6VTggg]{Off-Guard} for 1 round.

Success The target becomes off-guard against your attacks for 1 round.
