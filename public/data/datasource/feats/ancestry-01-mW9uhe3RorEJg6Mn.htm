Name: Nagaji Spell Familiarity Nom: Familiarité avec les sorts nagajis État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Either through study, exposure, or familial devotion, you have the magic of nagas bubbling in your blood. During your daily preparations, choose @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.4gBIw4IDrSfFHik4]{Daze}, @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.Yb0C1uLzeHrVLl7a]{Detect Magic}, or @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.pwzdSlJgYqN7bs2w]{Telekinetic Hand}. Until your next daily preparations, you can cast the chosen spell as an occult innate cantrip. A cantrip is heightened to a spell rank equal to half your level rounded up.
