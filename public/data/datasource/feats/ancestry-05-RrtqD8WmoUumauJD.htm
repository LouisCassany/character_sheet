Name: Mask Of Power Nom: Masque de pouvoir État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Your warmask sharpens your connection to the source of your mask's power. During your daily preparations, choose @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.4koZzrnMXhhosn0D]{Fear}, @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.R8bqnYiThB6MYTxD]{Phantom Pain}, or @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.Gb7SeieEvd0pL2Eh]{Sure Strike}.

Until your next daily preparations, you can cast this spell as a 1st-rank innate spell once per day; the spell's tradition is determined by the tradition tied to your warmask. You must be wearing your warmask to Cast the Spell.
