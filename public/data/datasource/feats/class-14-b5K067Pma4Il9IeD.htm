Name: Anchoring Aura Nom: Aura d'ancrage État: officielle -- Desc (en) --

Your aura hampers fiends' teleportation. Your aura attempts to counteract teleportation spells cast by fiends within 15 feet, using the spell rank and DC of your devotion spells.
