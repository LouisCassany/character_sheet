Name: Distracting Spellstrike Nom: Frappe de sort distrayante État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Requirements You're in Arcane Cascade stance and have a hand free.

You weave distracting magic with one hand to enhance your Spellstrike. Make a Spellstrike and @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.QNAVeNKtHA0EUw4X]{Feint} against the target of your Strike. Do this immediately before making the Strike, but after choosing your target.
