Name: Skull Creeper Nom: Crâne épouvantable État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You wear skulls to demoralize foes. You become trained in Intimidation and gain the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.xQMz6eDgX75WX2ce]{Intimidating Glare} skill feat. If you're already trained in Intimidation, you instead become trained in a skill of your choice. If you pay 50 gp for a @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.equipment-srd.Item.HYnwTFuLsFSsUAMG]{Splendid Skull Mask}, the mask grants you a +1 item bonus to Intimidation; its usage is worn (mask).
