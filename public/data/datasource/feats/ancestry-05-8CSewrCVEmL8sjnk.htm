Name: Nephilim Resistance Nom: Résistance du néphilim État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You resist energy like your extraplanar predecessors. Choose one of the following energy damage types: acid, cold, electricity, fire, or sonic. You gain resistance 5 to that damage type.

The damage type typically matches an extraplanar entity associated with your bloodline. For instance, an @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.ydgCsYsgqSkFWEDK]{Angelkin} might choose resistance to cold, while a @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.EXVHePH8alsTZ5TB]{Hellspawn} might choose resistance to fire.

Le type de dégâts correspond typiquement à l'entité extraplanaire associée à votre lignée.
