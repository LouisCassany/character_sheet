Name: Plant Shape Nom: Morphologie végétale État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You can take the shape of a plant creature. If you don't have untamed form, you can cast @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.zCcfPS4y5SrZzU2x]{Plant Form} once per day, heightened to the same rank as your highest-rank druid spell slot.

If you do have @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.feats-srd.Item.VaIHQzOE5ibmbtqU]{Untamed Form}, add the shapes listed in @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.zCcfPS4y5SrZzU2x]{Plant Form} to your @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.8RWfKConLYFZpQ9X]{Untamed Form} list, and whenever you're polymorphed into another shape using untamed form, you gain resistance 5 to poison.
