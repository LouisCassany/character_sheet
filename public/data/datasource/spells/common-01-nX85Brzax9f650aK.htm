You make the object @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.zJxUflt9np0q4yML]{Invisible}. This makes it @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.VRSef5y1LmL2Hkjf]{Undetected} to all creatures, though the creatures can attempt to find the target, making it @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.iU0fEDdBp3rXpTMC]{Hidden} to them instead if they succeed.
