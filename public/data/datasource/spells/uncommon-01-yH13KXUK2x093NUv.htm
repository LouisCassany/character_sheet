Name: Face in the Crowd Nom: Fondu dans la foule État: officielle -- Desc (en) --

While in a crowd of roughly similar creatures, your appearance becomes bland and nondescript. You gain a +2 status bonus to Deception and Stealth checks to go incognito among the crowd, and you ignore difficult terrain caused by the crowd. This counts as setting up a disguise for the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.AJstokjdG6iDjVjE]{Impersonate} use of Deception and you add your level even if you're untrained.

@UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spell-effects.Item.4iakL7fDcZ8RT6Tu]{Spell Effect: Face in the Crowd}

Heightened (3rd) The spell gains a range of 10 feet and can target up to 10 creatures.
