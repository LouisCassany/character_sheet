Name: Mushroom Patch Nom: Parterre de champignons État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You cause mushrooms to sprout in the area. The area becomes difficult terrain. When the spell is cast and at the beginning of each round, the mushroom's release a cloud of irritating spores. Creatures in the area must attempt a Fortitude save.

Critical Success The creature is unaffected.

Success The creature is @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.TkIyaNPgTZFBCCuh]{Dazzled} for 1 round. If the creature moves to outside area, they can spend 1 action to rub their eyes, removing the dazzled condition.

Failure The creature becomes dazzled for 1 round.

Critical Failure The creature becomes dazzled and @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.xYTAsEpcJE1Ccni3]{Slowed 1} for 1 round.

Heightened (3rd) The range increases to 90 feet and the area increases to a 20-foot-radius burst.

Heightened (6th) The range increases to 120 feet and the area increases to a 30-foot-radius burst.

Heightened (9th) The range increases to 150 feet, the area increases to a 40-foot-radius burst, and when a creature would become dazzled, they become @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.XgEqL1kFApUbl5Z2]{Blinded} instead.
