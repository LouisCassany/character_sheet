Name: Mantle of the Unwavering Heart Nom: Manteau du coeur inébranlable État: changé Changés: desc État d'origine: libre -- Desc (en) --

Vines and branches envelop you in an instant, growing a wild bloom of colors that open to reveal your new form. Upon Casting this Spell, pick two of the options below. As a single action, which has the concentrate trait, you can change one of your chosen abilities to a different option from the list.

  • Evergreen Vitality You gain fast healing 3 and a +2 bonus to saves against poison and disease. This effect has the healing and vitality traits.
  • Overwhelming Perfume (aura, olfactory) A cloud of potent floral scent surrounds you in a @Template[emanation|distance:15]. Creatures in the aura can't benefit from the scent ability and are @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.fesd1n5eVhpCSS18]{Sickened 1} as long as they remain in the aura. The sickness is a disease effect.
  • Towering Trunk You grow to a considerable height. Increase your size to Large. You're @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.i3OJZU2nk64Df3xm]{Clumsy 1}. Your reach increases by 5 feet (or by 10 feet if you started out Tiny), and you gain a grasping branch unarmed attack; this attack is in the brawling weapon group and deals @Damage[2d8[bludgeoning]] damage plus Grab.
  • Unyielding Will Your mind becomes incredibly resilient to change. You're immune to being @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.AdPVz7rbaVSRxHFg]{Fascinated}, and you get a +1 bonus to saves and DCs against mental effects.
