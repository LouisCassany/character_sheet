With the threat of more pain, you compel a creature you've recently harmed. You issue a command to the target, with the effects of the spell @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.aIHY2DArKFweIrpf]{Command}.If the target is @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.TBSHQspnbcqxsmjL]{Frightened}, @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.e1XGnhKNSQIm5IXg]{Stupefied}, or taking persistent damage, it takes a –2 circumstance penalty to the save.
