You create a replenishing protective bubble of fresh, temperate air around the target. The shell of air enables the target to breathe even underwater or in a vacuum, protects from severe cold and heat, and grants a +1 status bonus to the target's saves against inhaled dangers such as inhaled poisons and @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.MlpbeZ61Euhl0d60]{Toxic Cloud}.
