Name: Radiant Field Nom: Champ rayonnant État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You create an area of bright light. Creatures with @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.bestiary-ability-glossary-srd.Item.I0HYG0ctCLP5JRsW]{Light Blindness} that are @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.XgEqL1kFApUbl5Z2]{Blinded} by radiant field and remain in the area must attempt a Fortitude save at the start of their turns. On a failure, a creature remains blinded for 1 round; this is an incapacitation effect.

This spell also suppresses magical darkness of your radiant field spell's rank or lower.

Heightened (4th) Creatures seen through the area are @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.DmAIPqOBomZ7H95W]{Concealed} to creatures with light blindness, even those that are outside the area.

Vous créez une zone de lumière vive.
