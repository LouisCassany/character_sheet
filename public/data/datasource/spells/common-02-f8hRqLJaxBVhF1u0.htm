Name: Acid Arrow Nom: Flèche acide État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You conjure an arrow of acid that keeps corroding the target after it hits. Make a spell attack against the target. On a hit, you deal 3d8 acid damage plus @Damage[(floor(@item.rank/2))d6[persistent,acid]] damage. On a critical hit, double the initial damage, but not the persistent damage.

Heightened (+2) The initial damage increases by 2d8, and the persistent acid damage increases by 1d6.
