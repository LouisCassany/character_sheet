Name: Scouring Pulse Nom: Impulsion décapante État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Area 10-foot radius, 60-foot-tall cylinder

You inundate the area with concentrated vitality energy, which scours away the stain of undeath. Each creature in the area takes 6d8 vitality damage and must attempt a basic Reflex save. Creatures that fail the save also take @Damage[1d8[persistent,vitality]] damage. Creatures that take persistent vitality damage from scouring pulse are wreathed in a heatless halo of light. They emit bright light in a 5-foot radius preventing them from being @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.DmAIPqOBomZ7H95W]{Concealed}; if the creature is @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.zJxUflt9np0q4yML]{Invisible}, they're concealed while affected by scouring pulse, rather than being undetected.

Heightened (8th) The vitality damage increases to 9d8 damage, and the persistent vitality damage increases to @Damage[2d8[persistent,vitality]] damage. Creatures remain suffused in light for [[/r 1d4 #Rounds]]{1d4 additional rounds} after removing the persistent vitality damage.

Les créatures restent imprégnées de lumière pendant [[/r 1d4 #Rounds]]{1d4 rounds} supplémentaires après s'être débarrassées des dégâts de vitalité persistants.
