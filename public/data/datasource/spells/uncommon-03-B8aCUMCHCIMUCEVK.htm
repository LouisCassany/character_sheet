You call upon your element to propel you, improving your Speed depending on your element. This spell has your element's trait.

  • @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spell-effects.Item.iqtjMVl6rGQhX2k8]{Air} You gain a fly Speed equal to your Speed.
  • @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spell-effects.Item.qbOpis7pIkXJbM2B]{Earth} You gain a burrow Speed of 10 feet.
  • @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spell-effects.Item.q4EEYltjqpRGiLsP]{Fire} You gain a fly Speed equal to your Speed.
  • Metal You gain a burrow Speed of 10 feet.
  • @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spell-effects.Item.D0Qj5tC1hGUjzQc4]{Water} You gain a swim Speed equal to your Speed and can breathe underwater.
  • Wood You gain a climb Speed equal to your Speed.

Heightened (6th) You also gain a +10-foot status bonus to your Speeds.

Heightened (9th) The status bonus increases to +20 feet.
