Name: Stone Lance Nom: Lance de pierre État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You conjure a jagged lance of stone and then launch it at a foe. Make a spell attack roll against the target. On a hit, you deal 6d6 piercing damage and the lance impales the creature, giving it a -10-foot circumstance penalty to its Speeds unless it Escapes. On a critical hit, if the creature is on the ground, the lance also embeds into the ground and @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.eIcWbB5o3pP6OIMe]{Immobilizes} the creature until it Escapes. A creature that Escapes after being impaled takes @Damage[3[bleed]] damage. When the spell ends, the lance crumbles into dirt, freeing the target if it hasn't Escaped.

Heightened (+1) Increase the damage by 2d6 and the persistent bleed damage for Escapes by 1.
