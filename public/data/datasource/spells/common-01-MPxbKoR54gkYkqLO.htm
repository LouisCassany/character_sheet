Name: Gouging Claw Nom: Griffe creusante État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You temporarily morph your limb into a clawed appendage. Make a melee spell attack roll against your target's AC. If you hit, you deal your choice of 2d6 slashing damage or 2d6 piercing damage, plus @Damage[(@item.level +1)[bleed]] damage. On a critical success, you deal double damage and double bleed damage.

Heightened (+1) The damage increases by 1d6 and the persistent bleed damage increases by 1.
