Name: Personal Blizzard Nom: Blizzard personnel État: libre -- Desc (en) --

Your patron's breath becomes blizzard of obscuring, scouring ice that follows your target. The target attempts a Fortitude save.

Critical Success The target is unaffected.

Success The target takes @Damage[1d6[cold]] damage, and the spell ends.

Failure The target takes @Damage[1d6[cold]] damage and @Damage[1d6[persistent,cold]] damage. The persistent damage automatically ends when the spell ends. It is @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.DmAIPqOBomZ7H95W]{Concealed} to other creatures, and other creatures are concealed to it.

Critical Failure As failure, but both the cold damage and the persistent cold damage increase to 2d6.

Heightened (+1) The cold damage and persistent cold damage increase by 1 (2 on a critical failure).
