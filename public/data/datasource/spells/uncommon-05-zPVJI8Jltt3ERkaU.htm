Name: Boomerang Shot Nom: Tir boomerang État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You launch a curved length of wood at a foe that arcs around objects and obstacles to strike from an unexpected direction. Make a spell attack roll against the target's AC. This attack ignores the target's @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.DmAIPqOBomZ7H95W]{Concealed} condition and ignores all cover except greater cover. Heightened (9th) The damage increases to 12d10, and the attack ignores cover completely.
