You dance, @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.Bcxarzksqt9ezrs6]{Striding} up to half your Speed. At either the start or end of your Stride, you create an area of magical darkness in a 10-foot burst centered on your location. This area is filled with magical darkness that works the same way as that created by a @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spells-srd.Item.4GE2ZdODgIQtg51c]{Darkness} spell of the same rank. When you create the darkness, attempt a Performance check to dance, with a standard DC for your level, to determine the duration of the darkness.
