Name: Disappearance Nom: Disparition État: officielle -- Desc (en) --

You shroud a creature from others' senses. The target becomes @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.VRSef5y1LmL2Hkjf]{Undetected}, not just to sight but to all senses, allowing the target to count as @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.zJxUflt9np0q4yML]{Invisible} no matter what precise and imprecise senses an observer might have. It's still possible for a creature to find the target by Seeking, looking for disturbed dust, hearing gaps in the sound spectrum, or finding some other way to discover the presence of an otherwise-undetectable creature.
