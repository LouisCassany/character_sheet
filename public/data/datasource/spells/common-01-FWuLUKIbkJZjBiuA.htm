Name: Eat Fire Nom: Manger le feu État: changé Changés: desc État d'origine: libre -- Desc (en) --

Trigger You would take fire damage.

You consume and ingest fire, making it less harmful to you. You gain resistance 5 to fire damage until the end of the current turn. During the remaining duration of the spell, you can use the Belch Smoke action. The spell ends if you fall @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.fBnFDH2MTzgFijKf]{Unconscious}, inhale, or exhale (this includes speaking).

@UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spell-effects.Item.BGv44XBGtD4zOJBd]{Spell Effect: Eat Fire}

Belch Smoke 1 You exhale what remains of the smoke, ending the spell and creating a smoke cloud in a @Template[burst|distance:5] within 20 feet. All creatures within the smoke cloud are @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.DmAIPqOBomZ7H95W]{Concealed}, and all other creatures are concealed to them. The smoke lasts for 1 minute or until dispersed by a strong wind.

Heightened (+3) The resistance increases by 5.
