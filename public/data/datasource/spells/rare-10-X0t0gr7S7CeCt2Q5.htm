Name: Anima Invocation (Modified) Nom: Invocation d'anima modifiée État: libre -- Desc (en) --

When casting the modified version of the Anima Invocation, it instead draws upon the latent spiritual energies of sapient creatures within range of the spell without requiring their deaths, then uses this energy to bolster the connection between body and soul among all of the ritual's casters. This shift from an offensive effect to a defensive one results in a much less dangerous spell that helps to protect against Dahak.

@UUID[Compendium.pf2e.spell-effects.Item.0koqUuC1YW4F1l5z]{Spell Effect: Anima Invocation}

Critical Success You gain full access to your own potential for 1 day. You gain resistance 10 to all damage and a +4 status bonus to saves against effects created by evil creatures. Your Strikes gain the effects of the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.equipment-srd.Item.JQdwHECogcTzdd8R]{Ghost Touch} rune and deal an additional @Damage[1d10[spirit]] damage. You are immune to the soul-draining effect within Alseta's Landing.

Success You tap into your own potential for 1 day. You gain resistance 5 to all damage and a +2 status bonus to saves against effects created by evil creatures. Your Strikes gain the effects of the ghost touch rune and deal an additional @Damage[1[spirit]] damage. You are immune to the soul-draining effect within Alseta's Landing.

Failure You are unable to tap into your own spiritual essence, but you still gain immunity to the soul-draining effect within Alseta's Landing.

Critical Failure You botch the ritual, damaging your own spiritual essence. You gain @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.3uh1r86TzbQvosxv]{Doomed 1} or increase your doomed condition by 1 if you're already doomed. This doomed value decreases by 1 every week. This is a curse effect.

-- Desc (fr) --

Quand on incante la version modifiée d'Invocation d'anima, le rituel puise dans l'énergie spirituelle latente des créatures douées d'intelligence dans la portée du sort, sans provoquer leur mort. Le rituel utilise ensuite cette énergie pour renforcer le lien entre le corps et l'âme des incantateurs du rituel. Cette modification qui transforme un effet offensif en effet défensif donne lieu à un sort beaucoup moins dangereux qui permet de se protéger contre Dahak.
