Name: Revulsion Nom: Répulsion État: libre -- Desc (en) --

You can't voluntarily come within 10 feet of brandished garlic or a religious symbol of a non-evil deity. A creature must Interact to brandish garlic or a religious symbol for 1 round (similar to Raising a Shield). If you involuntarily come within 10 feet of an object of your revulsion, you gain the @UUID[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.sDPxOjQ9kx2RZE8D]{Fleeing} condition, running from the subject of your revulsion until you end an action beyond 10 feet of it. After 1 round of being exposed to the subject of your revulsion, you can attempt a @Check[will|dc:25] save as a single action, which has the concentrate trait. On a success, you overcome your revulsions for [[/r 1d6 #Overcome Revulsion Duration]]{1d6 rounds}, or 1 hour on a critical success.

En cas de succès, vous surmontez votre répulsion pendant [[/r 1d6 #Surmonter la répulsion]]{1d6 rounds} ou pendant 1 heure en cas de succès critique.
